WHO Regional Director on Pneumonia Outbreak in China 
 Jan.19 -- Takeshi Kasai, regional director for the Western Pacific at the World Health Organization, talks about the outbreak of a new SARS-like virus that’s killed two and sickened dozens in China. The surge in cases “is the result of increased searching and testing” for the novel corona virus among people sick with respiratory illness, the WHO said Monday. Kasai speaks on "Bloomberg Markets: Asia."
