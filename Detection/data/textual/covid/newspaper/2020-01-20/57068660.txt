Japan Firms Bracing for Pneumonia Outbreak in China - JIJI PRESS 
 Tokyo, Jan. 20 (Jiji Press)--Japanese companies operating in China are taking measures against a new type of coronavirus that has led to an outbreak of viral pneumonia in the inland China city of Wuhan.

    Mobile phone carrier Softbank Corp. <9434> has instructed local employees to work only at home, while Nippon Steel Corp. <5401> and electronics giant Sony Corp. <6758> told their employees not to make business trips to China that are not urgent.
