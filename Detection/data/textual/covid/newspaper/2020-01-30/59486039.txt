Morrison responds to WHO coronavirus announcement, QLD criticism 
 January 31, 2020. Prime Minister Scott Morrison talks to media in Sydney about the World Health Organisation’s classification of the coronavirus as a public health emergency. "Australia has been acting in advance of this decision…All of the issues - isolation, case management, contract tracing, prevention of onward spread, active surveillance, early detection - Australia has been doing,” Morrison says. Morrison defends charging evacuees money to leave, and says his government is in “constant” contact with all states after criticism from Queensland’s Premier Annastacia Palaszczuk.
