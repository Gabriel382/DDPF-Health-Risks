Coronavirus alert as first person-to-person spread in US after Illinois woman passes killer virus to husband 
 THE first person-to-person spread of coronavirus has been confirmed in America after an Illinois woman passed the deadly disease to her husband.

US health officials said the new patient lives with the woman, who was diagnosed with the virus after returning from Wuhan, China.
