Coronavirus now a global health emergency, World Health Organization declares 
 The World Health Organization has officially declared the coronavirus outbreak a public health emergency, a rarely used designation that will trigger a coordinated global response to the growing crisis.“The main reason for this declaration is not because of what is happening in China, but because of what is happening in other countries,” Tedros Ghebreyesus, the agency’s director-general, said at a news conference Thursday. ”Our greatest concern is the potential for the virus to spread to countries with weaker health systems, and which are ill-prepared to deal with it.”This is a developing story.
