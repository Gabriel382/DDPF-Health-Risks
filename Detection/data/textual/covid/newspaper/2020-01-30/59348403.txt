Coronavirus: "The data that's available has been very partial and inadequate" 
 Scientists in China have shared accounts and information that suggest the Coronavirus outbreak could be much worse than what authorities there state on the record, warns Dr. Stephen Morrison, senior vice president at the Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS). The director of its Global Health Policy Center says it's too early to draw firm conclusions on the danger of a virus for which a vaccine is a long way off.
