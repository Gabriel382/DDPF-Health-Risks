Two more cases of Coronavirus confirmed in Qld, Vic 
 Two more cases of the deadly Coronavirus has been confirmed in Australia bringing the national total to nine. Queensland Health reported a second case of the disease, isolating a 42-year-old Chinese in the Gold Coast University Hospital. She was a member of the same tour group the other confirmed Queensland case was travelling in. In Victoria, health authorities have identified a third virus patient as a woman in her 40s visiting from Hubei Province in China. She visited a GP and was taken to Royal Melbourne Hospital where she remains in isolation. The number of cases in China approached 8,000 with officials claiming 170 patients have died.
