Morocco's RAM airline temporarily suspends flights to China due to coronavirus - state news agency 
 RABAT, Jan 30 (Reuters) - Moroccan airline Royal Air Maroc (RAM) temporarily suspended its direct flights to China due to the coronavirus outbreak, state news agency MAP said on Thursday. 
RAM has launched on January 16 a direct link with three flights weekly between its Casablanca hub to Beijing. (Reporting by Ahmed Eljechtimi Editing by Chizu Nomiyama)
