Stocks close higher as rosy earnings offset coronavirus fears 
 U.S. stocks reversed course mid-afternoon to close the session higher as investors took heart from efforts from the World Health Organization to contain the Wuhan coronavirus outbreak. The Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, +0.43% added about 124 points, 0.4%, to close near 28,859, while the S&P 500 SPX, +0.31% closed about 10 points, 0.3%, higher, around 3,284. The Nasdaq was about 24 points, 0.4%, higher, closing near 9,299. Separately, shares of Tesla TSLA, +10.30% rocketed higher after beating forecasts, but UPS UPS, -6.75% shares slid after revenues missed expectations.
