What is Coronavirus and how to prevent it: All you need to know | India News - Times of India 
 NEW DELHI: In China, the death toll of the novel Coronavirus has jumped to 170 and more than 1,700 new infections have been reported by the government. Several global airlines, including Air India, British Airways, Lion Air and American airlines have suspended their flights to Chinese cities. Foreign nationals in hundreds have been evacuated from China. Countries are taking preventive measures and isolating the travellers arriving from China to screen them for flu-symptoms.
