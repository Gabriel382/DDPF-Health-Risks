patient tests negative for coronavirus 
 OTTAWA -- Ottawa Public Health confirms a patient in Ottawa tested negative for the coronavirus.

On Tuesday, OPH says it was made aware of an individual who was undergoing testing. Those test results came in negative Thursday afternoon. The indiviudal had been in self-isolation.

OPH says it is not aware of any confirmed or presumptive coronavirus cases in the Ottawa-area.

On Thursday, the World Health Organization declared a global health emergency over the coronavirus outbreak in China.

So far in Canada, there have been two confirmed cases in Toronto and one presumptive case in British Columbia.
