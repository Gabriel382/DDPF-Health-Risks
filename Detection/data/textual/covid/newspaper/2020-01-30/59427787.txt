First person-to-person transmitted case of coronavirus confirmed in US 
 The first case of person-to-person transmission coronavirus has been confirmed in the US. (Picture: File Photos/Getty Images)The first person-to-person transmission of coronavirus has been confirmed in the US, according to federal health officials.

Authorities said a patient contracted the virus after being in regular close contact with a woman in Chicago, who was the second confirmed case of coronavirus in the US.

The woman was diagnosed with coronavirus after returning to the US from Wuhan, China, which is known to be the epicenter of the disease.
