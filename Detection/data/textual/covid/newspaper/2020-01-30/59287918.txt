Kerala reports first confirmed coronavirus case in India 
 India has reported its first confirmed case of novel coronavirus in Kerala. The patient is a student studying in Wuhan University, China.

In a statement, the Press Information Bureau of India said the student is from Kerala.

"The patient has tested positive for novel coronavirus and is in isolation in the hospital. The patient is stable and is being closely monitored," the statement said.

The 2019-novel coronavirus (2019-nCoV) started in China's Wuhan city early December and has so far claimed more than 170 lives.
