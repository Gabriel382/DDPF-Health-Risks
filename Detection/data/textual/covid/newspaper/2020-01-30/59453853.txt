Coronavirus warning: UK threat increases as chief medical officers urge Government action 
 The medical officers have urged the Government to take action and "plan for all eventualities”. 
They did add, however, the risk to individuals has not increased despite the threat level being raised. 
Health experts made the announcement following a meeting with the World Health Organisation emergency committee in Geneva on Thursday night.
Despite the threat level now being increased to moderate, approximately 150 British national will return to the UK. 
The group had been stuck in the city of Wuhan - the centre of the outbreak - and been blocked from returning. 
They will board a flight leaving the Chinese city at 7am local time on Friday morning (11pm Thursday GMT).
