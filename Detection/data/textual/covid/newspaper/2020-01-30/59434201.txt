1st person-to-person spread of new coronavirus in US identified 
 The novel coronavirus has spread for the first time in the U.S. through person-to-person contact, the the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) announced today (Jan. 30) during a teleconference. The virus was confirmed in the husband of an Illinois woman who had contracted the infection after traveling to China.
