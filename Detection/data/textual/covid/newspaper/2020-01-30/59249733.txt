Tiger Air passengers urged to contact Queensland Health 
 Supplied video obtained Thursday, January 30, 2020 of Queensland Health Minister Steven Miles speaking to reporters in Brisbane after the first case of the lethal coronavirus was confirmed the state. Mr Miles says the man, a 44-year-old Chinese national, has been isolated in Gold Coast University Hospital. Mr Miles says the man travelled on Tiger Air flight TT566 from Melbourne to the Gold Coast on Monday. "Queensland Health is now working to get contact details from all passengers on that flight," Mr Miles says. Mr Miles urges anyone that was on the flight to contact Queensland Health.
