Coronavirus - 6,000 on lockdown on cruise ship after Chinese couple fall ill 
 About 7,000 passengers and crew are trapped on a cruise ship in Italy amid fears two holidaymakers  have coronavirus.

A husband and wife from China have been put in isolation on the Costa Smerelda, which could have Britons on board, after the couple came down with a fever and breathing difficulties.
