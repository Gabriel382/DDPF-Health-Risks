CDC confirms first case of person-to-person transmission in the U.S. 
 The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said Thursday that it has confirmed the first case of person-to-person spread of coronavirus in Illinois. There are now six confirmed cases of coronavirus in the U.S., two of which are in Illinois. The sixth case is the spouse of the first confirmed case in Illinois and did not travel to Wuhan, China, the city that first identified the novel coronavirus.
