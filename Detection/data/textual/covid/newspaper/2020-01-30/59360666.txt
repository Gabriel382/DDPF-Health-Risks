Flight bringing Brits back from coronavirus city in China will leave tonight 
 A flight carrying British nationals from Wuhan airport to the UK will leave China in a matter of hours.

The plane, expected to carry 200 Britons, will leave coronavirus epicentre Wuhan at 5am local time on Friday – which is 9pm tonight in the UK – Foreign Secretary Dominic Raab said.

The flight was initially supposed to leave this morning but was delayed as negotiations with Chinese authorities continued.
