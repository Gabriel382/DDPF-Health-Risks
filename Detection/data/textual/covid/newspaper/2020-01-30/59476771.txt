American Airlines pilots union sues company to stop China flights in wake of coronavirus outbreak 
 The union which represents American Airlines pilots said it sued the company Thursday in an attempt to immediately halt all service to China as the impact of the coronavirus spreads throughout the travel industry.

The Allied Pilots Association, which represents 15,000 American Airlines Group, Inc. (Nasdaq: AAL) pilots, filed the lawsuit in Dallas Country on Thursday and is seeking a temporary restraining order to immediately halt all American Airlines flights to China, the union said.
