Brit rescue flight from coronavirus-hit Wuhan will leave China tomorrow 
 A FLIGHT carrying British nationals from Wuhan airport in China to the UK will leave Wuhan on Friday, it was announced today.

The plane initially booked to evacuate hundreds of Brits from the coronavirus-hit region had initially been blocked from leaving.

But Foreign Secretary Dominic Raab today announced the flight would leave tomorrow, 5am local time.

More than 200 British citizens are currently trapped in the sealed-off city dubbed zombieland.
