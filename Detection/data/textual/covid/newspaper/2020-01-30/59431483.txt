How Tampa International Airport's most popular airlines are responding to the coronavirus threat 
 Some of Tampa International Airport's largest carriers are making changes to their flight schedules due to growing concerns over the coronavirus. 

Wednesday afternoon, Delta Air Lines joined United Airlines and American in temporarily reducing its number of weekly flights between the U.S. and China due to "significantly reduced customer demand prompted by global health concerns related to coronavirus."
