 Coronavirus: Why We Can’t Trust the Chinese Regime’s Data

In this episode of The China Report we speak with Dana Cheng, senior editor of China News at The Epoch Times, about why the official data on the coronavirus from the Chinese regime cannot be trusted, how censorship and delays in actions made the spread of the virus worse, and what the current situation is on the ground in Wuhan and across China.
