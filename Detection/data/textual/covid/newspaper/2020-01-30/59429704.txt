First person-to-person transmission of coronavirus confirmed in U.S. 
 Federal health officials have confirmed the first human-to-human transmission of the Wuhan coronavirus in the U.S.The Chicago patient is married to the woman who became the country’s second person to contract the pneumonia-like virus last week. Authorities said she had recently traveled to Wuhan, the Chinese city where the infection is believed to have originated.Details on the man’s condition was not immediately available.The latest case brings the total number of U.S. patients diagnosed with the illness to six.
