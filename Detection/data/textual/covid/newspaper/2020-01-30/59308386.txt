Russia closes 2,700-mile border with China to stop coronavirus 
 The Russian Prime Minister has signed an order to close the country’s far east border with China, it has been reported.

The drastic measure comes amid mounting panic over the pneumonia-like flu, which has so-far spread to over 15 countries.

President Vladimir Putin ordered officials to do all they can to prevent the spread of the new disease.

PM  Mikhail Mishustin, told his cabinet on Thursday:  ‘We will inform everyone about the border closures in the Far East as well as other measures taken by the [Russian] government’.
