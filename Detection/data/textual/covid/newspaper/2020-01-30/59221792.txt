Canada's chief public health officer says a coronavirus vaccine is 'probably' a year away 
 Canada's Chief Public Health Officer gave an update on the coronavirus to Canadian lawmakers earlier today.



Dr. Theresa Tam says although there is an 'international effort' underway to find treatments for the coronavirus it will 'probably' take at least a year to develop a vaccine.
