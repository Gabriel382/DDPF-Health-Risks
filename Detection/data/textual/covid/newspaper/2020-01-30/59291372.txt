India reports its first case of novel coronavirus 
 FILE PHOTO:  Medical staff with protective clothing are seen inside a ward specialised in receiving any person who may have been infected with coronavirus, at the Rajiv Ghandhi Government General hospital in Chennai, India, January 29, 2020. REUTERS/P. Ravikumar/File photo


(Reuters) - India on Thursday reported its first case of the novel coronavirus in Kerala, the government said in a statement. 
The patient has tested positive for novel coronavirus and is stable and in isolation at a hospital, the statement added.
