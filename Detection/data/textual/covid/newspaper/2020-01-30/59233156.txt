Wuhan coronavirus: Latest news on the global outbreak 
 In late 2019, reports emerged of a mysterious illness in China. It was identified as a new type of coronavirus, believed to have come from a seafood market in Wuhan, the capital of central China's Hubei province.

Thousands of cases have since been confirmed around the world, with more than 100 dead. Authorities are now trying to stop the virus spreading further, restricting millions of people from traveling and introducing strict quarantines.
