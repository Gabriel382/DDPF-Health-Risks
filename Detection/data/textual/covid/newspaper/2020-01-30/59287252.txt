Coronavirus in India: First case of fatal virus confirmed in Kerala 
 A person in Kerala, who was studying at Wuhan University in China, has been detected positive for Novel Coronavirus, news agency ANI has reported.

The condition of the patient is said to be stable and is being closely monitored by the doctors, the report said.

Novel coronavirus belongs to the Coronavirus family that causes illnesses ranging from the common cold to acute respiratory syndromes. It has emerged from a seafood and animal market in Wuhan city of China.
