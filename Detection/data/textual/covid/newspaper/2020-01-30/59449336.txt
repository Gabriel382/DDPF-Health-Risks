Coronavirus outbreak declared global public health emergency 
 The World Health Organisation (WHO) declared that the coronavirus outbreak is a global public health emergency.

This follows a meeting by the Emergency Committee on the novel coronavirus (2019-nCoV) under the International Health Regulations (IHR 2005).

The WHO said in a statement that globally 7818 confirmed cases have been reported, with 7736 being from China.
