Gold futures mark highest settlement since April 2013 
 Gold futures climbed Thursday to score their highest settlement since April 2013, as concerns surrounding China's coronavirus and its potential impact on the global economy boosted haven demand for the metal. April gold GCJ20, +0.79% rose $13.20, or 0.8%, to settle at $1,589.20 an ounce. That was the highest most-active contract finish since April 1, 2013, according to FactSet data.
