First coronavirus cases confirmed in Italy 
 Prime Minister Giuseppe Conte has announced there are two confirmed cases of coronavirus in Italy, the first two in the country since the emergency exploded.
Conte said Italy had closed air traffic to and from China.
"We have two confirmed cases of coronavirus in Italy," the prime minister told a news conference on Thursday, adding two Chinese tourist from China had contracted the virus.
On Thursday, the World Health Organisation declared that the coronavirus epidemic in China now constitutes a global public health emergency.
