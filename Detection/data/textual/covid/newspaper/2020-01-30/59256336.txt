Palaszczuk move to reassure over coronavirus 
 January 30, 2020. Queensland Premier Annastacia Palaszczuk speaks to reporters in Brisbane after meeting with health authorities following the state’s first confirmed case of coronavirus. Ms Palaszczuk says four tourists who were with the Chinese national who fell ill are being tested for the virus in the Gold Coast University Hospital. China’s women’s soccer team are also isolated in a Brisbane hotel and will not be allowed to leave until February 5. "Everybody is complying with what they need to do," Ms Palaszczuk says. "My key message today, to Queensland families, is... please listen to the authorities." (AAP Video/Michael Doyle)
