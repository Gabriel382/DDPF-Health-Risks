Montco biopharm firm's stock surges again on new coronavirus pact 
 Inovio Pharmaceuticals' stock soared once again Thursday morning after the Blue Bell company announced a collaboration with Beijing Advaccine Biotechnology Co. to advance the development in China of its potential vaccine against the recently emerged strain of coronavirus.

The Montgomery County company's stock was up 16% at $4.77 per share in premarket trading.

The new strain of the coronavirus has caused 170 deaths and more than 7,700 people have been infected, according to world health organizations.
