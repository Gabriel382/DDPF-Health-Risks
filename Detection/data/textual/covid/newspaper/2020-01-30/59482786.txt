Deadly day in Hubei as coronavirus kills a record high 42 patients 
 The daily death toll from the rapidly spreading coronavirus in China’s Hubei province hit a record high on Thursday, Hubei health authorities said, with 42 more fatalities.Of the newly reported deaths, 30 were in Wuhan, the epicentre of the outbreak, according to the Health Commission of Hubei province.Thursday’s toll brought the nationwide death total to 213.
