Local nonprofit sending aid to China in face of coronavirus outbreak 
 A Pittsburgh foundation is sending help to Wuhan, China, the city where the contagious and deadly coronavirus was first traced.

Brother's Brother Foundation, a 61-year-old aid agency that started more than 60 years ago to help end polio and smallpox, is sending donated medical supplies and protective equipment, according to a release.
