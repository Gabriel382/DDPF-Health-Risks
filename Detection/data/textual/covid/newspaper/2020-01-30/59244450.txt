NSW health updates on coronavirus 
 January 30, 2020. NSW chief medical officer Kerry Chant speaks to reporters in Sydney, saying no new cases of coronavrius have been reported in NSW. Of the four previously reported cases, two patients have been discharged “and the other two remain clinically stable”. Ms Chant says 20 people are currently being tested for the virus. “All of those individuals will be in isolation whilst we’re awaiting test results,” Ms Chant says. (AAP Video/Greta Stonehouse)
