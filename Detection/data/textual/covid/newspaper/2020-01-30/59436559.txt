Coronavirus outbreak: Ontario health officials provide update on response efforts 
 Ontario's Chief Medical Officer of Health Dr. David Williams and Associate Chief Medical Officer of Health Dr. Barbara Yaffe provided an update on Thursday, on the province's response efforts to contain the spread of the novel coronavirus.



There are two confirmed cases of the coronavirus in Ontario.
