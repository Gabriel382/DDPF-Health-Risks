Wuhan pneumonia outbreak: Japan confirms first case of infection 
 Japan has confirmed its first case of China coronavirus. The infected person is said to be a Chinese citizen who lives near Tokyo and had visited Wuhan, where the outbreak is believed to have started. The patient has recovered and been discharged. The Health Ministry said his family has not been infected, and there is very little possibility that the virus is spreading.
