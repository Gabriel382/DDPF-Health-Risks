China's National Health Commission Says 9 Deaths From Coronavirus Outbreak Confirmed 
 Chinese National Health Commission Vice President Li Bin said at least nine people died from a coronavirus outbreak on Monday, Reuters reported.

At least 13 Chinese provinces are affected with the virus, Li said.
