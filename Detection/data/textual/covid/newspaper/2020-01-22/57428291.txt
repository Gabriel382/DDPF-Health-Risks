Macao confirms 1st novel coronavirus case - People's Daily Online 
 MACAO, Jan. 22 (Xinhua) -- Novel Coronavirus Response and Coordination Centre of the Macao Special Administrative Region government announced on Wednesday that the first case of novel coronavirus has been reported in a hospital here.


	A 52-year-old woman traveling from Wuhan of central China came to Centro Hospitalar Conde de Sao Januario Tuesday afternoon and was diagnosed with novel coronavirus pneumonia.
