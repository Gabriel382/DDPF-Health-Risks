Travel stocks shake off coronavirus concerns 
 Hotel stocks are tracking higher after some concerns of a widespread outbreak of the Wuhan virus are tamped down.

Hilton Worldwide (HLT ), Hyatt Hotels (H ) and InterContinental Hotels Group (IHG ) are among the gainers.

Chinese airlines China Southern Airlines (ZNH ) and China Eastern Airlines (CEA ) are also higher on the day. On this side of the Pacific, U.S. airlines JetBlue (JBLU ), American Airlines Group (AAL ) and Alaska Air Group (ALK ) are outperforming on the day.
