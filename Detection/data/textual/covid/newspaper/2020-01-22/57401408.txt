 Fears of global epidemic as deadly virus is spread by flights from China

A woman and child take precautions in Daxing airport, BeijingNicolas Asfouri/Getty ImagesCities around the world were tightening health checks on international travellers last night amid growing fears that a respiratory disease sweeping through China could become a global epidemic.
 A study by the University of Hong Kong (HKU) estimated that the coronavirus had already reached 20 other mainland cities from Wuhan, where it was first identified last month, and that this spread had occurred in the first half of this month. Cases were confirmed yesterday in the United States, Taiwan and the Philippines. Thailand and Australia were investigating suspected infections. Japan, South Korea and Thailand already have cases.
