Hong Kong confirms first case of new Wuhan virus- local media 
 HONG KONG, Jan 22 (Reuters) - Hong Kong confirmed its first case of a new flu-like coronavirus on Wednesday following an outbreak in the central mainland China city of Wuhan, local broadcasters RTHK, Cable TV and TVB said, citing unnamed sources.

The outbreak, which began in Wuhan, has spread to more Chinese cities including the capital Beijing, Shanghai, and Macau and cases have been reported outside the country's borders, in South Korea, Thailand and Japan. (Reporting by Hong Kong newsroom; Writing by Marius Zaharia; Editing by Andrew Heavens)
