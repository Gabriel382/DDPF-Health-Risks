China coronavirus: Hong Kong to confirm first case 
 Hong Kong is likely to confirm its first infected case of a new coronavirus on Wednesday, a source has told the Post, involving a mainland Chinese tourist who took a high-speed train and had been to Wuhan – the epicentre of the outbreak.It is understood the patient is currently isolated and being treated in Queen Elizabeth Hospital in Jordan. The second test for confirmation is under way.He was detected to have fever when arriving at the high-speed rail terminus in West Kowloon on Tuesday…
