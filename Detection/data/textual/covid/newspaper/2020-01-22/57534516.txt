Why wild animals are a key ingredient in China’s coronavirus outbreak 
 Before its closure, exotic animals – from snakes to civet cats – were available at a wet market in the central Chinese city of Wuhan that is ground zero of a new virus killing people with pneumonia-like symptoms and infecting growing numbers of others around the world.The previously unknown coronavirus had infected 471 people in 17 Chinese provinces and regions as of Wednesday, with experts believing that it had jumped from wild animals for sale in the Huanan Wholesale Seafood Market.
