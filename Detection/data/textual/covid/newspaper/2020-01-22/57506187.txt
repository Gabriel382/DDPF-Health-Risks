Hong Kong, Macau report coronavirus infections 
 HONG KONG (Kyodo) -- Media outlets in Hong Kong and Macau on Wednesday reported the first confirmed cases in the territories of a new coronavirus that has spread in China.

The case in Hong Kong arrived from central China's Wuhan city, where the epidemic originated, the reports said, adding that the person has been hospitalized in isolation.

A Chinese businesswoman who arrived in Macau from Wuhan Sunday also tested positive after checking herself in to a hospital Tuesday, local authorities said. She is reportedly in stable condition.
