Thailand confirms second case of Wuhan coronavirus 
 Thailand has confirmed a second case of the mysterious coronavirus that first appeared in China's Wuhan city in December. This comes as it steps up checks on visitors arriving from China. Officials said there is no outbreak in Thailand and they are very confident of controlling the situation.
