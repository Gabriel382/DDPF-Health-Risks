China reports second death from Wuhan coronavirus 
 A second person has died after contracting the coronavirus found in the central Chinese city of Wuhan, as further reports emerged of suspected cases in nearby countries.A 69-year-old man died on Wednesday, Wuhan Health Commission said late on Thursday.
