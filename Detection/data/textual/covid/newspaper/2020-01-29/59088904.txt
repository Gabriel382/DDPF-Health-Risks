Coronavirus: Airlines suspend flights, some Starbucks, KFC, McDonald's locations closed in China 
 British Airways, Lion Air and Air Seoul have suspended all flights to and from mainland China, the first international airlines to do so. Air Canada, United Airlines and Cathay Pacific have all cut flights. Ikea, Starbucks, KFC and McDonald's have all closed locations in affected areas.
