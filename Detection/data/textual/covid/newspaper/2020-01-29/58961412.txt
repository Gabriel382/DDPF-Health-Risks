Logan To Begin Screening For Coronavirus: Reports 
 Logan Airport will start screening passengers for the fast-spreading coronavirus, according to reports. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention will soon institute the screening process, which will be taking place at some 20 airports around the country, according to ABC News.

Nine passengers on one flight that came into Logan from Hong Kong last Wednesday were screened as a precaution, but the airport has not been regularly screening.

Two people in New Hampshire who recently came back from China have symptoms, but the virus has not been confirmed. No cases in New England have been confirmed as of Tuesday night.
