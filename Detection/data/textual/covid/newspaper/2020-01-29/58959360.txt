Here are the major events scheduled for the day across the country, January 29 
 If we look at the significant events on Wednesday, all eye turn towards Delhi as the central Cabinet meeting will be held at 10.30 am today ahead of parliament sessions. Today's central cabinet has much importance as it is to be seen whether the union cabinet discusses the repeal of Andhra Pradesh Legislative council resolution sent to cabinet secretary on Tuesday. 
 
Here are the major events for the day 
 
 
 
Today in National 
 
 
 
The Union Cabinet is scheduled at 10:30 am to discuss the budget proposals and parliament sessions to begin from January 31. 
 
 
 
Today in sports 
 
 
 
The third T20 between India and New Zealand will take place today. The match begins at 12:30 pm at Hamilton Stadium in New Zealand. 
 
 
 
Today in Andhra Pradesh 
 
Chief Minister YS Jagan Mohan Reddy will hold a review meeting with the officials at 11 am to discuss the Housing lands in Visakhapatnam 
 
 
 
Telangana 
 
After a massive victory in the recently held Municipal elections, The Telangana Rashtra Samithi will elect its Mayor and deputy mayors for Karimnagar Corporation today.
