Korean Airlines to Suspend China Flights Due to Coronavirus 
 Korea's second-biggest carrier, Asiana Airlines, said it is suspending flights between Incheon and Guilin as well as Incheon and Haikou starting this Saturday. It is also halting flights between Incheon and Changsa from next week.

Similar measures have been taken by budget carriers Air Seoul, Eastar Jet, Jeju Air and Jin Air. For people who cancel or postpone flights to China, some airlines are waiving the related fees.
