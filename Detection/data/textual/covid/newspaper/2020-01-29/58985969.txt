Chinese under watch for novel coronavirus dies in San Lazaro Hospital 
 MANILA, Philippines — A 29-year-old Chinese man, who is under investigation for suspected novel coronavirus infection has died while undertaking treatment at the San Lazaro Hospital in Manila, an official from the hospital announced on Wednesday.

In a press briefing, San Lazaro Hospital director Dr. Edmundo Lopez said the victim who is from Yunnan, China, died due to pneumonia.

Lopez also noted that the samples from the Chinese victim were already sent to the Research Institute for Tropical Medicine (RITM) in Muntinlupa for an initial test for suspected novel coronavirus infection.

“Specimens have been taken. May test na natira ang RITM (There are still tests in RITM) for detection of coronavirus,” Lopez said.

Lopez said the Chinese patient had varying symptoms including enlarged lymph nodes.
