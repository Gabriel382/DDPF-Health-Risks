Global Corporate Giants Limit China Travel in Response to Coronavirus 
 Multinational companies such as JPMorgan Chase  Co., Apple Inc. and Kraft Heinz Co. are suspending business trips to China amid concerns about an outbreak of the fast-spreading coronavirus.
  JPMorgan banned travel into and out of mainland China and imposed a two-week, work-from-home quarantine on anyone who had been there in recent weeks.
