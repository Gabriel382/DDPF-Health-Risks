Coronavirus update: Health advice recommends a highly cautious approach 
 January 29, 2020. Chief medical officer Professor Brendan Murphy speaks to the media in Canberra at a briefing with the Health Minister Greg Hunt about the latest information regarding the coronavirus outbreak. Professor Murphy says there has been a case in Germany where a man who did not display symptoms of the coronavirus, appears to have infected 4 other people at a seminar. Mr Murphy says the latest health advice is that Australia should take a highly precautionary approach. People who have returned to Australia already should self-isolate for 14 days. Mr Hunt says Australia's approach is the most forward leading and the most cautious in the world. (AAP Video/Marc Tewksbury)
