Etela Rajender to take stock of novel coronavirus preparedness 
 Health Minister Etela Rajender to hold a high level review on Coronavirus preparedness with senior officials of the health department on Wednesday.
 
Also, he would enquire about department arrangements and health camps at Medaram for Samakka Saralamma jatara. 
 
Meanwhile, the three member Central team visited Gandhi hospital to inspect the 40 bed isolation ward for admission and treatment of nCoV suspected cases and positive cases from the city.
