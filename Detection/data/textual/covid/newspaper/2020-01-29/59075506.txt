Utilities Stocks Set for Biggest Monthly Gains Since 2016 
 Utilities stocks have been among this month’s winning bets as the widening coronavirus outbreak has sent investors scurrying for safety.
  The sector has risen 5.8% in the SP 500 so far in January, on pace for its best month since June 2016, when the U.K.’s Brexit referendum sparked a broad market panic. The SP 500 itself is up 1.4% for the month.
