Guntur: Returnee from China tested for virus 
 Guntur: Doctors conducted tests on Nadendla Venkata Subbaiah, resident of Chilakaluripet town on Wednesday after his return from China. Tests were conducted in view of coronavirus that was rampant in China.
The Medical and Health Department has been conducting test on those returning from China. 
The doctors came to know of Venkata Subbaiah's China visit on being alerted by officials. 
Primary Health Centre physician T Gopi Naik conducted tests on Venkata Subbaiah. The doctor certified that Venkta Subbaiah was healthy. He said he would submit the report to District Medical and Health Officer Y Yasmin.
