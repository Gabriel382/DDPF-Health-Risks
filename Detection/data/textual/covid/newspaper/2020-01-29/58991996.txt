Chinese under watch for novel coronavirus dies in San Lazaro Hospital 
 San Lazaro Hospital director Dr. Edmundo Lopez said the victim, who is from Yunnan, China, died on Wednesday at 7 a.m. The patient was admitted last Monday.

“Early this morning around 7 a.m., We have person under investigation, hindi pa confirmed for nCoV, na namatay. [He]was admitted last Monday,” Lopez said in a press briefing.

(Early this morning around 7 a.m., We have a person under investigation, who was not confirmed for nCoV, has died. He was admitted last Monday]
