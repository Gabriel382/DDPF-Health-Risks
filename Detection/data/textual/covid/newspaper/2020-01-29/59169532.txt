Delta (NYSE: DAL) 'monitoring' coronavirus situation, has not canceled flights 
 Raleigh-Durham International Airport's largest carrier by passenger volume, Delta Air Lines Inc. is standing pat on flights to and from China, for now.

But the same can't be said for its peers at RDU, United Airlines and American Airlines.
