World Athletics consider new venue for World Indoor Championships as coronavirus concerns rise 
 World Athletics is starting to consider alternative venues for the World Indoor Championships in March in anticipation that China may pull the plug on the event because of the coronavirus outbreak.
Officials from the global governing body are continuing to take guidance from the World Health Organisation while remaining in dialogue with the national federations. As yet no country has officially withdrawn their team but there is growing concern at UK Athletics as well as other federations.
British Airways has suspended all direct flights to mainland China and the UK government has warned against all but essential travel to the country as the death toll caused by the rapidly spreading disease grew to 132.
