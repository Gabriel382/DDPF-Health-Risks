Honolulu airport to begin screening for coronavirus 
 Federal health officials have expanded the number of U.S. airports conducting coronavirus screenings from five to 20, with Honolulu’s Daniel K. Inouye International Airport among them.

On Monday, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention also issued a warning that travelers should avoid all nonessential travel to China.
