Starbucks, Haidilao close China stores in response to coronavirus 
 Starbucks said on Wednesday it would close more than half of its stores in China, and hotpot chain Haidilao International Holding said it would temporarily close all of its locations in the mainland as the deadly Wuhan coronavirus worsened.China is the Seattle-based coffee chain’s largest market outside the United States and has been seen as a major area of growth in its international business by Starbucks chief executive Kevin Johnson.
