Sudan suspects two infections with the new coronavirus: minister 
 KHARTOUM (Reuters) - Sudan suspects that two of its citizens who returned to the country from China are infected with the new coronavirus, the information minister told Reuters. 
One of the pair, a man and a woman, had been in the region of the Chinese city of Wuhan, the epicenter of the virus, Said Faisal Saleh. Both are under medical checks.
