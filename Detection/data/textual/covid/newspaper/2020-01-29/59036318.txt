Britons on coronavirus airlift from Wuhan WILL be quarantined 
 People returning to the UK from the Chinese city of Wuhan on a coronavirus evacuation flight will be quarantined at a military base for two weeks.

Up to 300 British expats may be brought home on an emergency flight tomorrow when the Government charters a plane to rescue them.

Wuhan, the city at the centre of the deadly coronavirus outbreak, is currently on lockdown with no public transport and no flights out of the city.

The UK Government will tomorrow charter a plane to evacuate citizens from Wuhan, the locked-down city at the centre of China's coronavirus outbreak. British Airways (pictured) today announced it would stop flying between London and China for the forseeable future
