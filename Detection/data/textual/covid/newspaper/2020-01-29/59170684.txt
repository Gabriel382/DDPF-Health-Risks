Boeing joins battle to protect Chinese from fast-spreading coronavirus 
 The Boeing Co. said Tuesday it's donating 250,000 medical-grade respiratory masks to alleviate supply shortages in China as public health officials battle the spread of the deadly coronavirus.

Boeing said its masks will be provided to local Chinese public health officials working to fight the spread of the virus in Wuhan City, Hubei Province, and Zhoushan, Zhejiang Province.
