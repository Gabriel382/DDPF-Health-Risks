PM announces Christmas Island to be used to quarantine Aussies from Wuhan 
 January 29, 2020. The Prime Minister Scott Morrison speaking at a press conference in Canberra this morning about the latest measures the government will be implementing in dealing with the coronavirus and its impacts. Travel advice has been upgraded to advise against all travel to China. Dealing with those Australian in Wuhan and Hubei province, those evacuated from Wuhan will be sent to Christmas Island for quarantine period of up to 14 days. (AAP Video/Marc Tewksbury)
