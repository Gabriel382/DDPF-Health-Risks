Wuhan virus: UAE confirms coronavirus case in family from China 
 DUBAI (REUTERS) - A new coronavirus case has been diagnosed in members of a family from China's central city of Wuhan, United Arab Emirates state news agency WAM on Wednesday (Jan 29) quoted the country's health ministry as saying.

It was not immediately clear how many people were infected with the virus that has killed 132.

The health ministry and government media office did not immediately respond to an e-mailed request for comment.
