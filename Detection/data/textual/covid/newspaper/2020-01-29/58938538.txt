Confirmed new-coronavirus cases top 5,000 in China 
 BEIJING (Kyodo) -- Confirmed cases of patients infected with a new pneumonia-causing coronavirus have topped 5,000 in China, with the death toll increasing to 131 across the country, local health authorities said Wednesday.

The virus is believed to have begun spreading last month in Wuhan, the capital of Hubei Province, around 1,000 kilometers south of Beijing. The city is a major business hub with a population of 11 million.
