PM's presser on coronavirus 
 January 29, 2020. The Prime Minister Scott Morrison and the Foreign Minister Maris Payne speak to the media in Canberra about the government's plans to deal with the hundreds of people still in Wuhan at the epicentre of the coronavirus. Consular officials are on their way to Wuhan to help facilitate the evacuation of people from China. Evacuees will be quarantined at Christmas Island for period of up to 14 days. The PM says he says the states can be cautious regarding school attendance. (AAP Video/Marc Tewksbury)
