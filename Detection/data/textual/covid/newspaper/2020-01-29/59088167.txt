Johnson & Johnson starts developing vaccine for coronavirus 
 FILE PHOTO: The Johnson  Johnson logo is displayed on a screen on the floor of the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) in New York, U.S., May 29, 2019. REUTERS/Brendan McDermid/File Photo  GLOBAL BUSINESS WEEK AHEAD


(Reuters) - Johnson  Johnson said on Wednesday it is working on  developing a vaccine for the new coronavirus that has killed more than 100 people in China.  
The company also said it was donating its HIV drug Prezcobix to China for use in research to find a solution against the outbreak. 
Reporting by Manas Mishra in Bengaluru; Editing by Bernard Orr
