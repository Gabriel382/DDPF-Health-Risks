Coronavirus toll reaches seven as Gold Coast man is treated in isolation 
 The number of confirmed cases of the deadly coronavirus in Australia has risen to seven as a 44-year-old Chinese man was rushed into isolation for treatment on the Gold Coast overnight. In Melbourne, a second case has been confirmed with a man in his 60s testing positive for the potentially deadly virus. Health authorities said the man visited 'the House of Delight' restaurant at Glen Waverly before falling ill. Anyone who dined there have been told to look out for symptoms. Chinese authorities said the virus has infected nearly 6,000 and killed 132 people.
