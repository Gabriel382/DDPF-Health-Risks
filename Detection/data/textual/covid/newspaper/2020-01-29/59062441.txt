Atlanta, Chinese companies to collaborate on new coronavirus vaccine 
 An Atlanta biotech company has signed an agreement with a vaccine developer in Wuhan, China, to jointly develop a vaccine against the new coronavirus.

GeoVax Labs Inc. (OTC: GOVXD) said it has signed a letter of intent with Wuhan, China vaccine developer BravoVax to jointly develop a vaccine against the new coronavirus.
