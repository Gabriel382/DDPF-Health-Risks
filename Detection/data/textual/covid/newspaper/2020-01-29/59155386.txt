Seventh case of coronavirus confirmed in Australia 
 Confirmed cases of the deadly coronavirus in Australia has risen to seven after Queensland health authorities confirmed the state's first case. A 44-year-old Chinese national is being kept in isolation at Gold Coast University Hospital. A second case of coronavirus has also been confirmed in Victoria with four cases from New South Wales. Chinese authorities now say the virus has infected nearly 6,000 and killed 132 people. Image: News Corp Australia
