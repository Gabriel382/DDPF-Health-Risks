Charter Planes to Evacuate Koreans from Wuhan 
 The government said Tuesday that four chartered flights to Wuhan on Thursday and Friday will evacuate around 700 Koreans who remain at the epicenter of the coronavirus outbreak.

Vice Foreign Minister Lee Tae-ho told reporters, "We have identified around 700 Korean citizens in Wuhan who wish to return to Korea. The exact day could change depending on negotiations with China."
