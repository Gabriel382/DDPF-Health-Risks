MEDIA-China's Shenzhen city starts clinical research on HIV drugs for coronavirus treatment - Securities Times 
 BEIJING, Jan 29 (Reuters) - -- Source link: bit.ly/3aR1OFe -- City of Shenzhen in southern China has started clinical research on HIV drugs to treat coronavirus cases, the country's state-backed financial news outlet Securities Times reported on Wednesday, citing an official from the local bureau of the National Health Commision. -- Note: Reuters has not verified this story and does not vouch for its accuracy (Reporting by Cheng Leng, Lusha Zhang and Se Young Lee)
