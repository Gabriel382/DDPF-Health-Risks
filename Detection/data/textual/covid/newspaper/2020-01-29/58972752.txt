NSW Health testing 16 people for coronavirus, issues warning on social media post 
 January 29, 2020. NSW chief health officer Kerry Chant tells media in Sydney 16 people aged from two to 66 are currently being tested for coronavirus. She said this is a result of medicos broadening testing. Four confirmed coronavirus cases remain in the state. Chant warned social media users not to spread fake news about how the virus is contracted, confirming coronavirus infection was not possible through food.
