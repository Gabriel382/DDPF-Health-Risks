Delta 'monitoring' coronavirus situation, has not canceled flights 
 Delta Air Lines Inc. is standing pat on flights to and from China, for now.

As of Wednesday morning, the Atlanta-based airline has not made any changes to its operational schedule over coronavirus concerns, but it is monitoring the situation.
