Coronavirus: MLA Roja assures to rescue Telugu employees from Wuhan 
 MLA RK Roja has assured the parents of the employee who were trapped in Wuhan due to cancellation of flights to other countries. The arrivals and departures from Wuhan were cancelled in the wake of Coronavirus spreading across the world rapidly. 
 
The parents of employees who met Roja on Wednesday has said that their children who are working TCS are unable to return homes. The fast-spreading nCoV is sending shivers down the spine of the parents. Knowing this the parents were in a panic and moving pillar to pillar for the rescue of their wards. 
 
However, with Roja's assurance, if bringing them back, the parents are but relaxed. It remains to be seen how China government would respond to the situation.
