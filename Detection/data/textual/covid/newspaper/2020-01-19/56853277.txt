China coronavirus: Beijing breaks its silence, but only to ‘deny rumours’ 
 China’s central government has broken its silence on the Wuhan pneumonia outbreak with an online post aimed at “defying the rumours” surrounding the spread of the new coronavirus but without providing any new facts or figures on the issue.In a poster published on social media and its own website on Saturday night, the Chinese Centre for Disease Control and Prevention said the new virus was not Sars (severe acute respiratory syndrome), a 2002-03 outbreak of which killed more than 700 people…
