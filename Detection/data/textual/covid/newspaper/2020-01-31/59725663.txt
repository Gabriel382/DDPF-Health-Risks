Leaving Wuhan: Ben is evacuated to the UK and taken to two week quarantine zone 
 Irish teacher Ben Kavanagh has been evacuated from Wuhan and has landed in the United Kingdom.



He documents his flight from China, as well as his police-escorted bus ride to Merseyside, where UK nationals face two weeks of quarantine.
