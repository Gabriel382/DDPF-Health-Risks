WHO call to arms against virus needs global response to help China 
 The World Health Organisation’s decision to declare the new coronavirus a global public health emergency is a call to all nations to try and stop the spread of the pathogen across borders. But doctors said Beijing’s handling of the outbreak is unlikely to change because the country has already taken unprecedented measures to contain it.The UN health agency has yet to issue any advisory against travel to China, as it did during the severe acute respiratory syndrome (Sars) epidemic in 2002-03…
