Tokyo, (UrduPoint / Pakistan Point News - 31st Jan, 2020 ) :Japan is advising its citizens to avoid non-essential travel to all of China, over the risks of a deadly coronavirus outbreak, Prime Minister Shinzo Abe said Friday.
Japan had already raised its travel alert for Hubei province where the outbreak began, warning against any travel to the region.
