Third Minnesota Coronavirus Sample Sent To CDC 
 MINNEAPOLIS (WCCO) – The Minnesota Department of Health sent one coronavirus sample to the Center for Disease Control early this week.

This is the third sample from Minnesota sent off for testing. The other two tested negative, the CDC confirmed Monday.
