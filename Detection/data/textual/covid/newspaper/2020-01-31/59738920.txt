Baidu +4.2% on raised Q4 outlook 
 Baidu (NASDAQ:BIDU) updates its Q4 revenue guidance from RMB 27.1B-28.7B to RMB 28.3B-28.9B.

The company expects net income attributable to Baidu to come in between RMB 6.2B and 6.7B.

Baidu reschedules its Q4 report due to the coronavirus outbreak to February 27 with the earnings call at 8:15 PM ET.

BIDU shares are u after hours to $128.75.
