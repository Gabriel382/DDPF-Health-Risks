Nine people in Australia have the coronavirus: Health minister confirms 
 Supplied video obtained Friday, January 31, 2020, of Minister for Health Greg Hunt speaking at a press conference at Parliament House on Friday. Hunt says there are now nine confirmed cases of coronavirus in Australia. "Two in Queensland, Three in Victoria and Four in New South Wales," he says. "One positive development is that in NSW, two of the four patients that have been declared have now been released." (AAP VIDEO/Supplied/ParlView)
