Albanese backs Palaszczuk on federal government's coronavirus response 
 January 31, 2020. Labor leader Anthony Albanese backs Queensland Premier Annastacia Palaszczuk's criticism of the federal government's response to coronavirus. Albanese says details of people who travelled from Wuhan into Australia - before the flights were stopped - are not being passed onto some state leaders. (AAP Video/Michael Wade/Greta Stonehouse)
