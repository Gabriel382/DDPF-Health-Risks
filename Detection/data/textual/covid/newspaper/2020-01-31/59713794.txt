Two more employees of German car supplier get coronavirus 
 FRANKFURT (Reuters) - German car parts supplier Webasto [WEBA.UL] said on Friday that two more of its employees have tested positive for the coronavirus, making for a total of seven who have contracted it.  
One of the employees is German in Germany, while the other is Chinese in Shanghai. Both are in hospital undergoing treatment, Webasto said.
