Gov't to Send 2nd Evacuation Flight to Wuhan Late Friday 
 South Korea will send another evacuation plane to China late Friday to bring home citizens stranded in the city of Wuhan where the coronavirus has started as well as the surrounding areas in Hubei Province.


A Foreign Ministry official said on Friday afternoon that Beijing has given the green light for the evacuation and that another plane will leave for China later in the day.


The flight is expected to take off from Gimpo International Airport at 8:45 p.m.
