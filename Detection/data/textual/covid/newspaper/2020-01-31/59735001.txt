US government declares coronavirus emergency 
 The US government declared the new coronavirus a public health emergency and will bar entry to the US to foreigners travelling from China to prevent the disease from spreading in the country.The new measures, announced by Health and Human Services Secretary Alex Azar on Friday, will require US citizens, permanent residents and immediate family members arriving in the country from China’s Hubei province – the epicentre of the contagion – to be quarantined for 14 days starting Sunday at 5:00pm…
