The Coronavirus Blame Game, Explained by Chris Chappell 
 In this episode of The China Report we speak with Chris Chappell, host of China Uncensored, about how officials in China are trying to pass on blame for who is responsible for the spread of the coronavirus. We also discuss the history of the CCP’s cover-ups, which rumors on the virus can be proven or disproven, and explore the Chinese regime’s propaganda.
