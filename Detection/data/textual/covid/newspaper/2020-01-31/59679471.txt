Delta Air Lines to temporarily suspend all U.S. to China flights from Feb. 5 through April 30 
 Delta Air Lines Inc. DAL, -1.82% said Friday it is suspending all U.S. to China flights from Feb. 6 through April 30 due to concerns about the coronavirus that has sickened about 9,700 people and killed at least 213. The last China-bound flight operated by Delta will leave on Feb. 3 and the last return flight will take place Feb. 5. Delta shares were down 1.9% Friday, but are up 13% in the last 12 months, while the S&P 500 SPX, -1.11% has gained 20%.
