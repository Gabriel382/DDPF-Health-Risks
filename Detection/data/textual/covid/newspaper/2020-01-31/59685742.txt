P&G takes coronavirus precautions 
 Procter & Gamble hasn’t experienced disruptions to its business in China as a result of the outbreak of a deadly new strain of coronavirus, but the company is taking precautions to ensure the health of thousands of employees who work in the region or travel there as part of their job.
