 CPEC project halted in S Punjab amid oronavirus fear

MULTAN: In wake of growing coronavirus fear the ongoing work on the project under China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC) in South Punjab has been halted on Thursday.
According to details the screening process of 71 Chinese engineers and their security personnel is underway to diagnose any kind of virus in their bodies.
