Armenia suspends visa-free travel for Chinese citizens over virus fears -deputy PM 
 MOSCOW, Jan 31 (Reuters) - Armenia will suspend its visa-free travel regime for Chinese citizens from Feb. 1 until March. 31, over the coronavirus outbreak, Deputy Prime Minister Tigran Avinyan wrote on social media on Friday. (Reporting by Maria Tsvetkova, Writing by Alexander Marrow; editing by John Stonestreet)
