American ceases all China flying in wake of coronavirus outbreak 
 American Airlines will stop all flying to China immediately as fear of the coronavirus continues to impact the travel industry. 

An American Airlines Group Inc. (Nasdaq: AAL) spokesperson said Friday morning the carrier would halt flying to China from all U.S. airports starting Friday and the halt would last through March 27.
