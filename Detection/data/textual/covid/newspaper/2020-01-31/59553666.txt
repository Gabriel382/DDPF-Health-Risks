CPC allocates 108 mln yuan for coronavirus control - People's Daily Online 
 BEIJING, Jan. 30 -- The Organization Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) has allocated 108 million yuan (about 15.57 million U.S. dollars) to prevent and control the novel coronavirus epidemic.


	The money was appropriated from CPC membership dues to help frontline medical personnel as well as grassroots-level Party members, according to the ministry.


	Primary-level Party organizations will receive financial aid to purchase medicine and resources for epidemic prevention and control, said the ministry, adding that infected Party members and individuals who are in difficulty would also receive subsidies.
