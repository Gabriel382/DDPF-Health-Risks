White House bans recent foreign travelers to China & will quarantine US citizens who were near coronavirus epicenter 
 THE coronavirus outbreak prompted the White House to institute a 2-week entry ban on any foreign national who traveled to China.

Donald Trump's administration had been reportedly considering the measure as the deadly infection continues to spread in Wuhan and around the world.
