Topline: The U.S. State Department on Thursday told Americans not to travel to China due to public health concerns from the coronavirus outbreak. 



 The increased travel alert is the most severe warning U.S. officials have issued yet in relation to coronavirus. 

 The department said that Americans currently in China should consider departing using commercial means.
