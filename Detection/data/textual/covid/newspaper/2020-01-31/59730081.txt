Hyderabad: 80 year old with Swine Flu admitted in Gandhi Hospital 
 Hyderabad: When everyone's focus is around Coronavirus scare and suspected cases in Hyderabad for the last ten days or so, a Swine Flu positive patient was admitted to Gandhi hospital in Hyderabad on Friday. 
 
The 80-year-old patient from Yadadri-Bhongir district was first treated at a private hospital and later was admitted in Gandhi. He is being treated in the Swine Flu isolation ward.
