United Airlines cancels all flights to mainland China 
 United Airlines on Friday said it is cancelling all flights to and from mainland China starting Feb. 6 as the coronavirus crisis in the world’s most populous nation worsens by the hour.  

United said it will continue to operate one daily flight between San Francisco International Airport (SFO) and Hong Kong (HKG). Hong Kong sits on an island connected by bridge to mainland China.
