Delta, American suspend all U.S. to China flights as coronavirus spreads 
 Delta and American airlines on Friday said they will temporarily suspend all U.S. to China flights due to growing concerns over the coronavirus. 

American's suspension is effective immediately and runs until March 27 while Delta said its suspensions runs Feb. 6 through April 30.
