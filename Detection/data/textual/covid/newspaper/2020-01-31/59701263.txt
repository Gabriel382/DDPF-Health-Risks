Public health expert weighs in after coronavirus declared a global health emergency 
 The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the coronavirus a global health emergency after the virus has quickly spread and infected more than 8,000 people in the world.



Las Vegas-based Dr. Christina Madison is a public health expert, who says this will help in allocating proper resources to help with the outbreak.
