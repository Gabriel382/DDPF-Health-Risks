BREAKING: Patients under investigation for nCoV now at 31 
 MANILA, Philippines — The number of patients under investigation for suspected infection of the novel coronavirus acute respiratory disease has increased to 31.

This was confirmed by Health Undersecretary Eric Domingo in a press briefing on Friday.

“As of 12 noon today, we have two more patients under investigation, which brings the total to 31,” he said in a press briefing.

On Thursday, Health Secretary Francisco Duque said there are 29 patients under investigation on suspicion that they have contracted the virus.
