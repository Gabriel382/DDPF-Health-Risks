 For Hong Kong people, the memories of the Sars outbreak are still fresh. In 2003 there were 1,755 Sars cases and 299 deaths (according WHO). With the threat of the coronavirus outbreak declared a global emergency, Hong Kongers are taking all precautions. Most people are wearing masks, others goggles or double masks. Airports and the subway MTR system are screening passengers for high temperatures. People are buying up masks, hand sanitiser and even food in bulk
