Update: Delta suspends all U.S. to China flights 
 Delta announced Friday it will temporarily suspend all U.S. to China flights beginning Feb. 6 through April 30 "due to ongoing concerns related to the coronavirus." 

"Between now and Feb. 5, Delta will continue to operate flights to ensure customers looking to exit China have options to do so," the airline said in a news release.
