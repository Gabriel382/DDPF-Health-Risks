France's Pasteur Institute Foundation hoping to develop coronavirus vaccine 
 A man wearing a face mask rides a bicycle on a bridge in front of the financial district of Pudong in Shanghai, China January 31, 2020. REUTERS/Aly Song


PARIS (Reuters) - France’s Pasteur Institute Foundation is looking into developing a vaccine to treat the coronavirus, an official at the institute said on Friday. 
Christophe D’Enfert also told reporters the vaccine could be made available in 20 months’ time.
