Three confirmed cases of coronavirus in NSW, five undergoing testing 
 NSW Health Minister Brad Hazzard has confirmed an additional three people are undergoing testing for the deadly coronavirus in the state. Three others – who were already confirmed to have the disease – have been placed in isolation at hospital to prevent the further spread of the disease. Speaking to reporters on Sunday, Mr Hazzard said the three will find out the results of their pathology testing this evening. Two others have since been cleared of the disease.
