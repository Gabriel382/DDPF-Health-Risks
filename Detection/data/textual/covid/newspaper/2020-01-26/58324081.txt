Coronavirus outbreak: Toronto health official says contact with others low in ‘presumptive’ case | Watch News Videos Online 
 Dr. Eileen de Villa with Toronto Public Health said on Saturday that the man who was showing symptoms of coronavirus is believed to have had “very little” contact with others, and those family members who have been in contact have put themselves into self isolation. She said they’re also working on assessing the flight the patient came from Wuhan and his history to take appropriate action.
