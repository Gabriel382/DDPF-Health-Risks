China says virus situation ‘grave’ as Lunar New Year curtailed 
 WUHAN, China --- President Xi Jinping warned Saturday that China faced a "grave situation" as authorities raced to contain a virus that has killed 54 people and caused a drastic scale-back of Lunar New Year celebrations.

The world's most populous country, which is scrambling to contain the disease that has infected nearly 1,300 people and overwhelmed health facilities, is building a second field hospital and closing more travel routes.
