The last train had left Wuhan. We had yet to get out of this ghost town 
 Advice on how to survive novel coronavirus, given by a middle-aged woman outside Wuhan train station: “I heard there are measures you can take, like you can smoke or drink alcohol to keep safe because there is nicotine inside tobacco.”
 She was there to send her young nephew back to Beijing. “My brother is very concerned that his son is here.” The station was close to empty, however: a trickle of people where there should have been a torrent.
 If they had known what was to come, they would have been crowding the turnstiles to get out.
