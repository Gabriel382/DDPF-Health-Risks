Priti Patel: UK taking precautions and measures against coronavirus – video 
 Priti Patel said the UK was implementing the 'right measures' to try to prevent coronavirus reaching the UK, in an interview for Sophy Ridge on Sunday.

When asked about reports of an evacuation of more than 200 Britons trapped in Wuhan, China, where the virus originated, the home secretary said the UK was 'looking at all the options', adding there were still no confirmed cases of coronavirus in the UK
