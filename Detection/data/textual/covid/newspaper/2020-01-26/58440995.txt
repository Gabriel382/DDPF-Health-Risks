China says coronavirus can spread before symptoms show as death toll grows to 80 & 5th US case is confirmed 
 The death toll from the 2019-nCoV coronavirus outbreak in China has reached 80 with hundreds of new infections detected. Meanwhile, US public health officials have confirmed new cases of the coronavirus in Los Angeles and Arizona.

    
With 24 new death reported in Hubei Province – the epicenter of the outbreak – the overall death toll from the novel coronavirus in China now stands at 80, with over 2,400 confirmed cases countrywide.
