Playing possum and storming the pitch from the dugout — how Middlesbrough robbed Birmingham City of victory 
 One of the new and interesting ways in which modern players can stop the opposition scoring against them is to play possum: lie on the ground immobilised as if stricken with the coronavirus so that the referee, ever more mindful of health and safety considerations, stops whatever attacking move is being prosecuted by the opposing team and calls for a drop ball. This seems to be happening more and more often and I am not sure that it is necessarily improving the game we all love.
