China coronavirus death toll rises to 54 with 300+ new cases confirmed in outbreak epicenter Hubei 
 Chinese authorities have confirmed 13 more deaths and 323 new cases of coronavirus across Hubei province, bringing the toll to over 1,700 infected and 54 deceased nationwide –and several dozen cases across 10 other states.

    
The number of those who had beaten the virus and were discharged from hospitals has in the meantime increased to at least 85 people by Sunday morning. With over  1,000 confirmed infections in Hubei province alone, nearly 7,000 people remain under increased medical supervision as potentially dangerious cases of “close contact.”
