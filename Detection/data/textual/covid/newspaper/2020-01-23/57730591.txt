DAVOS (UrduPoint News / Sputnik - 23rd January, 2020) The World Economic Forum (WEF) in Davos is closely monitoring the situation around the outbreak of Novel Coronavirus in China but not yet declaring it an emergency, WEF press secretary Maxwell Hall told Sputnik.
On Wednesday, the World Health Organization convened an extraordinary committee meeting to discuss whether the situation should be declared a global emergency but postponed its decision until Thursday.
"We're taking it seriously. We are actively monitoring the situation and we have measures in place for anyone showing symptoms of fever," Hall said.
"We're following advice from the World Health Organization's (WHO) emergency committee which announced on Wednesday that it will not yet declare a global emergency over the new virus," he continued.
According to the organizers, the Chinese delegation at the WEF comprises over 100 people.
