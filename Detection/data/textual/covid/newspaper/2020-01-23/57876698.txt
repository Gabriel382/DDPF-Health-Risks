Student At Texas A&M University Treated For Possible Coronavirus 
 COLLEGE STATION (CBSDFW.COM) – The Brazos County Health Department notified Texas AM University that a student may have a possible case of novel coronavirus.

The university confirmed the possibility in a tweet Thursday afternoon.



 

In a news release, Texas AM said it continues to work with its Student Health Services and the Brazos County Health District.

Officials have described the immediate health risk to the campus community as low.
