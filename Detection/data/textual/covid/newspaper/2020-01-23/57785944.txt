Airlines and miners on alert over virus fears 
 UK stocks followed their Asian counterparts lower in early dealing in London as investors continued to fret over the potential ramifications of the coronavirus outbreak in China.
 Having managed to shrug off most of the concerns in yesterday’s session, stock markets in Asia fell sharply overnight.
 China’s SSE Composite index suffered its worst day in almost eight months as it dropped 2.8 per cent, the Nikkei in Japan lost 1 per cent and Hong Kong’s Hang Seng index slipped 1.5 per cent.
