Iran is screening passengers from China for symptoms of coronavirus: Iran health official 
 DUBAI (Reuters) - Iran is screening passengers from China at Tehran’s Imam Khomeini international airport for symptoms of coronavirus, the head of the center for infectious disease control at Iran’s health ministry said on Thursday, according to the semi-official Fars news agency.  
Fars did not provide any additional details on what the screening entailed.
