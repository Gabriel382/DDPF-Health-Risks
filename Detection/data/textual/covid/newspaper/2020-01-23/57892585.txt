Kuwait to thermal screen passengers 
 Kuwait says passengers arriving from countries affected with coronavirus will be thermally screened
The Kuwait ministry of health told the state news agency on Thursday that thermal cameras had been added to border crossings.
The ministry also said it was following the coronavirus developments confirming the readiness of all related ministry sectors to deal with the virus and to stop its outbreak.
"Airport's clinics and isolation rooms are well equipped and thermal cameras are added to border crossings" the statement said.
