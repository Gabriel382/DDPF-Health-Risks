Virus killing mostly the elderly, those with previous health problems 
 Almost half of the 17 people killed by the Wuhan coronavirus so far were aged 80 or over and most of them had pre-existing health problems, according to China’s health authorities. Children have been infected, but are not highly susceptible to the virus, they said.Details of the fatalities released on Thursday showed the youngest person was 48 and the oldest 89.All of those who died – 13 men and four women – were from the central province of Hubei, and treated in hospitals in its capital, Wuhan…
