Wuhan coronavirus lockdown sends airline stocks tumbling 
 Airline stocks tumbled on Thursday along with broader Hong Kong and China markets, after the government placed a travel ban on Wuhan city, the origin of a spreading new coronavirus strain that has infected hundreds and led to 17 deaths.Wuhan, a major transportation hub and the capital of Hubei province, has been in lockdown since 10am on Thursday morning, after the government shut down all public transport in and out of the city in an effort to contain the virus outbreak.
