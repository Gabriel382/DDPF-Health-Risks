CANADA STOCKS-TSX opens lower on weakness in energy stocks 
 Jan 23 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index opened lower on Thursday, pressured by losses in energy stocks as fears that the spread of a new coronavirus may hit fuel demand sent oil prices lower. 
* At 9:31 a.m. ET (14:31 GMT), the Toronto Stock Exchange’s SP/TSX composite index slipped 0.2% to 17,564 points. (Reporting by Medha Singh in Bengaluru; Editing by Aditya Soni)
