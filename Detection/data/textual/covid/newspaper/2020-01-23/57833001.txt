Commodities Fall Alongside Global Stocks With Coronavirus Spreading 
 Commodities from oil to copper extended a recent slide Thursday with a deadly virus spreading and disrupting travel in China, the latest sign that investors are bracing for a hit to economic growth in the world’s largest consumer of raw materials.
