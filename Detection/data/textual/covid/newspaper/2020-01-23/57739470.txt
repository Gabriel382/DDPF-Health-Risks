BEIJING (UrduPoint News / Sputnik - 23rd January, 2020) Public transport will stop working from midnight on Thursday in the city of Huanggang, China's Hubei Province to prevent the further spread of the new type of coronavirus, the administration of Hubei's second largest city said in a statement Thursday.
"From 24:00 on January 23, city buses, long-distance automobile passenger transportation will be temporarily suspended, railway traffic in the outbound direction is temporarily suspended. Residents should not leave the city without special need," the statement said.
