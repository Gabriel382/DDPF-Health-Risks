Canada better prepared for coronavirus, says SARS doctor 
 Dr. Michael Gardam, chief of staff at Toronto's Humber River Hospital, was on the front lines of the city's SARS outbreak. He says Canada is much better prepared for the new coronavirus than it was for SARS in 2003.
