Earnings today - What to watch 
 Earnings season continues in earnest today as several heavyweights report before and after the bell.

Investors will be watching for how Intel is working to combat market share loss, especially to AMD, while Procter & Gamble's strong organic sales momentum is expected to have continued.

Both American Airlines and Southwest will also report quarterly results after their stocks were hit this week due to concerns that the coronavirus outbreak in China would dent international travel.
