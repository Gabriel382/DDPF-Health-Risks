Beijing's Forbidden City to be closed to tourists amid virus outbreak: People's Daily 
 FILE PHOTO: Tourists visit the Forbidden City in central Beijing, China July 31, 2018. REUTERS/Jason Lee


BEIJING (Reuters) - Beijing’s Palace Museum, also known as The Forbidden City,   will be closed to tourists from Saturday as China battles the outbreak of a coronavirus, the official People’s Daily reported on Thursday.  
The reopening of what is the capital city’s most famous tourist attraction is pending further notice, the newspaper reported.
