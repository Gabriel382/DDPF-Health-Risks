Chinese told to comply with airport checks after woman ‘dodged’ screening 
 Chinese embassies have urged citizens travelling overseas to comply with airport health checks put in place in response to the coronavirus outbreak.It comes after a woman from Wuhan – the central Chinese city where the outbreak began – posted photos of herself at a restaurant in France on social media, saying she had been feeling ill when she travelled to the country so she evaded temperature screening at the airport.The Chinese traveller wrote that she had flu-like symptoms before she left…
