China's Hainan province confirms its first fatality from coronavirus: state tv 
 SHANGHAI (Reuters) - China’s Hainan province confirmed on Monday that an 80-year-old woman has died after being infected with the coronavirus, marking the province’s first fatality in the rapidly spreading outbreak, according to state broadcaster CCTV.
