         (Reuters) - U.S. stocks opened more than 1% lower on Monday, on concerns about the financial fallout of a fast-spreading coronavirus outbreak in China.
 
The Dow Jones Industrial Average () fell 447.24 points, or 1.54%, at the open to 28,542.49. The SP 500 () opened lower by 48.31 points, or 1.47%, at 3,247.16. The Nasdaq Composite () dropped 222.45 points, or 2.39%, to 9,092.46 at the opening bell.
