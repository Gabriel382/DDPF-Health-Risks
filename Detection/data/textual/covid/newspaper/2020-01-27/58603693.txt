A.O. Smith assists with aid to fight coronavirus in China 
 A.O. Smith Corp. is joining international companies assisting China in its fight against the deadly coronavirus. 

Milwaukee-based A.O. Smith donated and installed 50 air purification units in local hospitals in China, according to the company's director of corporate communications Tami Kou. Twenty units went to Gulou Hospital in Nanjing and an additional 30 went to Tongji Hospital in Wuhan. A.O.
