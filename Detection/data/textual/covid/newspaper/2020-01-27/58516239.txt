Global Stocks Slide On Coronavirus Fears 
 Stocks and crude oil tumbled Monday as the detection of infected coronavirus patients in the U.S., Australia and France led to escalating concerns about the containment and potential economic impact of the dangerous outbreak in China.
 Futures linked to the Dow Jones Industrial Average index dropped over 1%. In Europe, the Stoxx Europe 600 retreated 1.7%, led by declines in the U.K. and France. The Chinese yuan slumped 0.8% against the U.S. dollar in offshore trading.
