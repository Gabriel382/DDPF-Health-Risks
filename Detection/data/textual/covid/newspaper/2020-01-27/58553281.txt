Yum China -7% as virus impact factored in 
 Yum China (NYSE:YUMC) trades lower after KHC and Pizza Hut locations are closed by the restaurant operator in Wuhan, China as the coronavirus outbreak continues to worsen.

Restaurant traffic in general in China is expected to be lower this month due to concerns over the virus.

Shares of Yum China are in premarket trading today and are off more than 12% since the virus was first reported.
