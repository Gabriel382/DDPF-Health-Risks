The coronavirus is 'gripping Australia' 
 The Courier-Mail’s Damien Tomlinson says the deadly coronavirus is “something that has blown up in days and is certainly gripping Australia”. Nearly 2,000 people have contracted the virus in China where 56 are confirmed to have died. Fellow panelist, Sky News commentator Scott Emerson said, “we are seeing schools isolating students” and "they are not talking about the economic impact," of the virus. “You do worry,” Mr Emerson said, “how much bigger this will get”.
