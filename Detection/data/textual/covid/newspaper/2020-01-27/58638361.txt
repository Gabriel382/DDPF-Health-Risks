United Airlines closely watching coronavirus crisis as stock falls 
 Executives at United Airlines are closely monitoring the rapidly developing coronavirus crisis in China.

Meanwhile, United's stock tumbled about 5 percent in midday trading Monday as stocks across the board were down amid concerns about what impact the coronavirus might have on the global economy.
