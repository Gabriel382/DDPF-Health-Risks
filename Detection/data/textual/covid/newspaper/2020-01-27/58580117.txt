CANADA STOCKS-TSX falls 1% at open on Coronavirus fears 
 Jan 27 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index opened lower on Monday as investors fled risky assets on growing fears about the economic impact of the coronavirus outbreak in China. 
* At 9:35 a.m. ET (14:35 GMT), the Toronto Stock Exchange’s SP/TSX composite index was down 163.11 points, or 0.93%, at 17,402.23. (Reporting by Medha Singh in Bengaluru; Editing by Amy Caren Daniel)
