China's Tencent asks staff to work from home until Feb 7 due to virus 
 FILE PHOTO: People visit Tencent's booth at the World 5G Exhibition in Beijing, China November 22, 2019. REUTERS/Jason Lee


SHANGHAI (Reuters) - Chinese gaming giant Tencent Holdings Ltd (0700.HK) has requested staff to work from home until Feb. 7 to protect employees from the spread of the coronavirus, the company confirmed on Monday.
