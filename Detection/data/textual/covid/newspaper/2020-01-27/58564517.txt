China official says food supplies ample in virus-hit Wuhan 
 SHANGHAI (Reuters) - Food supplies including meat, eggs, and milk in the Chinese city of Wuhan are ample, its Communist Party chief Ma Guoqiang said on Monday. 
Wuhan is the epicenter of an outbreak of the new coronavirus and has been largely sealed off by the government to contain its spread.
