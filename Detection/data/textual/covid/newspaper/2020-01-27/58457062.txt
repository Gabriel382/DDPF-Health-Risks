China confirms youngest coronavirus patient yet 
 There are 68 confirmed cases of coronavirus in Beijing, according to the city’s health authority, including the youngest known case in the world so far: a 9-month-old baby girl.The virus continues to spread from its epicenter in Wuhan, China, infecting over 2,000 people worldwide and killing more than 50 Chinese people. On Sunday, three new cases were reported in the U.S., bringing the total number infected in the country to five. All five patients had recently visited Wuhan, and are currently hospitalized. The CDC has warned Americans not to visit Wuhan, and says it’s investigating over 100 possible cases of the virus in the country.“(We’re) getting calls all day, all night and that’s what we want,” director of National Center for Immunization and Respiratory Diseases Nancy Messonier told reporters on Sunday. “We want people to have a high index of suspicion and call if they have a patient they are concerned about.”
