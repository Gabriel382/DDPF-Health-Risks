Elbit rated Buy at Jefferies 
 While most of the market is under pressure this morning - on fears of the spreading coronavirus - Elbit Systems (NASDAQ:ESLT) is managing to hold its ground.

Jefferies initiated coverage on the stock at Buy, with a $185 price target, implying a 20% increase from its last price.

Analyst Sheila Kahyaoglu cites the potential for above average market growth, expecting a mid-single digit organic expansion through 2021.
