Four new tests for coronavirus in Qld 
 Four people are being tested for the deadly coronavirus in Queensland.
A spokeswoman for Queensland Health confirmed four new tests were being conducted on Monday.
"Through its Forensic and Scientific Services branch, Queensland Health are testing four patients for suspected coronavirus," she said.
"Biologists are working around the clock with results expected in the coming hours. "
There have been no positive test for coronavirus in Queensland.
The disease began in the central China city of Wuhan in December last year and has killed at least 80 people.
