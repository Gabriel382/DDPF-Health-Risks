China's NDRC to invest 300 million yuan for construction of hospitals in Wuhan: Xinhua 
 An aerial view shows the construction site of a new hospital dedicated to treating patients with coronavirus, in Wuhan, Hubei province, China January 24, 2020, in this still image taken from video. China News Service/via REUTERS TV


SHANGHAI (Reuters) - China’s National Development and Reform Commission (NDRC), the country’s state planning agency, plans to invest 300 million yuan ($43.5 million) for the construction of hospitals in Wuhan serving patients affected by the coronavirus, official news agency Xinhua reported on Monday. 
Reporting by Josh Horwitz and Winni Zhou; Editing by Tom Hogue
