NSW Health confirms fifth coronavirus case in Australia 
 January 27, 2020. NSW Health Minister Brad Hazzard and NSW Chief Health Officer Dr Kerry Chant speak to media in Sydney about a fifth confirmed case of coronavirus in the country. The 21-year-old woman is the fourth person to test positive in NSW along with three men in the state and one in Victoria. She arrived on China Eastern flight MU749 on Thursday, the last flight out of Wuhan to arrive in Sydney.(AAP Video/Michael Wade)
