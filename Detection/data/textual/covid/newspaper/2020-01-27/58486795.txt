Entry ban on Hubei visitors to Hong Kong will not be fully effective: minister 
 Hong Kong’s health minister has admitted the government’s entry ban on visitors from Wuhan would not be fully effective as the city’s top officials prepared to discuss new measures against the spread of the deadly coronavirus on Monday.A steering committee convened by Chief Executive Carrie Lam Cheng Yuet-ngor decided late on Sunday to bar from entering Hong Kong all Hubei residents and those who had visited the Chinese province within 14 days.
