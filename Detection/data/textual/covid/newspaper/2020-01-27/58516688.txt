Sensex closes 458.07 points down, Nifty settles at 12,119; Dr Reddy’s Labs, M&M, Cipla gain 
 Amid fears of an outbreak of a deadly virus Coronavirus in China, the Sensex on Monday (January 27) closed 458.07 points down or 1.10% at 41,155.12, and the broader Nifty ended down 129.30 points or 1.06% at 12,119. Major gainers on the Nifty were Dr Reddy’s Labs, M&M, Cipla, UltraTech Cement and Eicher Motors, while Vedanta, Tata Steel, JSW Steel, Hindalco and IndusInd Bank were among major losers.
