Travel sell-off hits Latin American carriers 
 Latin American airlines are reeling on broad concerns over global travel amid the coronavirus outbreak.

The weak day for the sector arrives even though no incidents of the new strain of coronavirus have been reported in South America.

Gol Linhas (GOL ), Avianca Holdings (AVH ), LATAM Airlines (LTM ), Azul (AZUL ) and Copa Holdings (CPA ) are all lower on the day.
