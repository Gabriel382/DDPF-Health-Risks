Hartsfield-Jackson will be one of two airports screening for coronavirus 
 Centers for Disease Control and Prevention officials said Tuesday that Atlanta would be one of two airports that will be adding health screening for a coronavirus originating out of China.

The screening will begin at Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport and Chicago's O'Hare International Airport, the CDC said.

The first U.S. case of the virus was confirmed Tuesday, in Washington state.
