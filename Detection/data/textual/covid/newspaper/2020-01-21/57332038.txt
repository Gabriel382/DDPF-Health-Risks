CDC expected to announce first U.S. case of coronavirus: report 
 The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention is expected to announce Tuesday afternoon that the first case of Wuhan coronavirus has been reported in the U.S., CNN reported, citing a federal source. The virus, first identified in Wuhan, China, has killed six people and infected more than 300 people. CNN said a media briefing is scheduled for later Tuesday.
