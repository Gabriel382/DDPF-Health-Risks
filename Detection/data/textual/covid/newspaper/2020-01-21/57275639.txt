China coronavirus MAPPED: Where has Coronavirus struck in China? 
 So far, six people are reported dead as a result of contracting the Coronavirus.
The illness causes a type of pneumonia and its symptoms include fever and difficulty in breathing, which are similar to many other respiratory diseases.
The official infection total, as of January 20, is 291 with 270 confirmed cases in Hubei province, home to Wuhan.
But this figure could be far higher, according to experts, who have suggested as many as 1,700 people may be carrying the coronavirus.
