Coronavirus in U.S. could impact cleaning stocks 
 Companies that sell disinfectants and cleaning products are on watch after the CDC says a traveler from China carried the coronavirus to Seattle.

Clorox (CLX ) and Reckitt Benckiser's (OTCPK:RBGLY) Lysol brand have seen a sales lift in the past when concerns over national virus outbreaks have occurred. Prestige Brands (PBH ) and Grainger (GWW ) are also in the mix.
