Brisbane man being tested for highly contagious coronavirus 
 There are reports a Brisbane man is being tested for the highly contagious coronavirus. Queensland Health said he was being held in isolation inside his home while he awaited his test results - which are due at the end of the week. Australian authorities are on high-alert, with border force taking extra precautions with passengers flying direct from Wuhan in China - where the virus is believed to have originated - to Sydney.
