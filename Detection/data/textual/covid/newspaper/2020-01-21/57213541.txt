Death toll from coronavirus rises to six as fears outbreak could spread grow 
 Six people have died from coronavirus in China’s central Wuhan city, where the outbreak first spread, the city’s mayor has confirmed.

Zhou Xianwang told state television a total of 258 cases had been confirmed in the city by the end of Monday.

Separately, China’s eastern Zhejiang provincial health authority said a total of five cases of new coronavirus were confirmed as of noon today.
