Scientists want to cut off Wuhan from the rest of the world to fight the spread of the deadly virus gripping the city 
 A Chinese government scientist has said that people should avoid coming in and out of Wuhan to stop a deadly coronavirus gripping the city from spreading further, The Washington Post reported.The pneumonia-like virus has killed six people and infected nearly 300 more, Reuters reported, citing Chinese authorities.
