CDC expected to announce first case of Wuhan coronavirus in U.S.: reports 
 The U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention is expected to announce Tuesday the first case of Wuhan coronavirus in the U.S., according to reports.The mysterious virus, which has killed at least six people and sickened hundreds of others in China, was reportedly detected in Washington state, according to the New York Times.No details about the person carrying the virus were immediately known.CDC officials were expected to hold a news conference Tuesday afternoon.This is a developing story.
