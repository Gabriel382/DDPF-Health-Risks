First case of Coronavirus confirmed in the US 
 A traveller from China is the first person in the US to be diagnosed with the potentially deadly Coronavirus. The American citizen, who is in his 30s, is in a good condition in hospital near Seattle after returning from China in the middle of last week, according to health officials. They said he did not show any symptoms when he arrived in the US on Wednesday, but then contacted doctors on Sunday when he began to feel unwell. The man is not considered a threat to medical staff or the public. Image: AP
