Paris, (UrduPoint / Pakistan Point News - 25th Jan, 2020 ) :Two cases of the coronavirus have been "confirmed" in France, the first in Europe, French Health Minister Agnes Buzyn said on Friday.
The first case involved a patient in hospital in the southwestern city of Bordeaux while the other was in Paris, the minister told reporters.
