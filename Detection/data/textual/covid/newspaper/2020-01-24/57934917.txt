Hong Kong, (UrduPoint / Pakistan Point News - 24th Jan, 2020 ) :Hong Kong shares rose slightly in Friday morning trade as the market stabilised following a week of investor nerves over China's coronavirus outbreak.
The Hang Seng index was up 0.11 percent, or 31.33 points, to 27,940.45 at lunch.
