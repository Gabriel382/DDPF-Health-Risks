Coronavirus: Wuhan in lockdown as residents barred from leaving Chinese city 
 China has barred the 11 million residents of Wuhan from leaving the city as it looks to prevent the spread of the potentially deadly coronavirus. The city will stop outgoing travel from the airport, train station and other public transport services
