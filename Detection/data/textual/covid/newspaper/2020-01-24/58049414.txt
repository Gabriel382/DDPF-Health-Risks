US testing 12 patients for coronavirus including Texas A&M student 
 US health officials are testing 12 patients across the country for the new, mysterious coronavirus that has been spreading across the globe.

This includes a male patient, identified as a student at Texas A&M University, who had traveled from Wuhan, China, where the virus originated.

Several people in California are also being examined to see if they have the virus that resembles SARS.

It comes on the heels of doctors revealing 43 people came into contact with US patient zero who is being quarantined in Washington state.
