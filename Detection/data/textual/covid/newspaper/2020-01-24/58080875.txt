CDC confirms second case of Wuhan coronavirus 
 (Reuters) - The U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention on Friday said a traveler from Wuhan, China had been diagnosed in Illinois, Chicago with the coronavirus. 
The agency said it has 63 patients under investigation from 22 states, with 2 confirmed positive cases and 11 negative, so far.
