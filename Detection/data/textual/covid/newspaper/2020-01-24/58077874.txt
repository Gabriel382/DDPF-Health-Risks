Wuhan virus: China city at centre of outbreak gets $390 million loan to help fight virus 
 BEIJING (REUTERS) - The China Development Bank has granted a 2 billion yuan (S$390 million) emergency loan to the city of Wuhan to help with virus prevention and treatment work, the city's government said in a statement on Friday (Jan 24).

Wuhan is at the centre of an outbreak of a new coronavirus in which at least 26 have died so far, most of them in the city.
