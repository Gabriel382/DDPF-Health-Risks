Coronavirus crisis: Great Wall of China CLOSED due to outbreak panic 
 Authorities have shut off one of the major access routes to the popular tourist destination to try and prevent the spread of the virus, which has claimed the lives of 26 people and infected more than 800 others.
The Badaling section is the best-preserved and most complete part of the famous Wall.
BNO news wrote on Twitter: “Badaling, the most popular section of the Great Wall of China, will be closed to prevent the spread of coronavirus.”
