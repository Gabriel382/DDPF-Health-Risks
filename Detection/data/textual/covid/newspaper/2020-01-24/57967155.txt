Coronavirus fears close Disneyland as cities put on lockdown over outbreak 
 Shanghai Disneyland is closing until further notice over fears the deadly SARS-like coronavirus could infect visitors and staff.

The virus has killed at least 25 people and infected more than 600 others during an outbreak in China, where a number of cities are on lockdown.

In a statement, the Shanghai Disney Resort said: "In response to the prevention and control of the disease outbreak and in order to ensure the health and safety of our guests and cast, Shanghai Disney Resort is temporarily closing Shanghai Disneyland, Disneytown including Walt Disney Grand Theatre and Wishing Star Park, starting January 25, 2020."
