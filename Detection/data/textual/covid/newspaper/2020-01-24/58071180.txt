CANADA STOCKS-TSX edges up at open as coronavirus fears ease 
 Jan 24 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index opened higher on Friday, hovering near an all-time peak, after worries over the coronavirus outbreak in China eased. 
* At 9:31 a.m. ET (14:32 GMT), the Toronto Stock Exchange’s SP/TSX composite index was up 23.78 points, or 0.13%, at 17,645.56. (Reporting by Medha Singh in Bengaluru; Editing by Aditya Soni)
