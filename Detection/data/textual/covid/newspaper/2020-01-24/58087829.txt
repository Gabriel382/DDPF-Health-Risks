First coronavirus case confirmed in Chicago 
 The CDC confirmed the first reported case of coronavirus in Chicago. (Picture: File Photo/Getty Images)The first case of the deadly coronavirus has been confirmed in Chicago, according to the CDC.

Health officials say a woman in her 60s was recently diagnosed with the mysterious disease after she returned home from Wuhan, China on January 13.

Her diagnosis marks the second confirmed case of coronavirus in the United States.
