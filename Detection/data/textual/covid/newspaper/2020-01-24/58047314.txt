'Too early to say how serious coronavirus is' says WHO expert – video 
 At a UN briefing in Geneva, WHO spokesman says it is too early to draw conclusions on the severity of the coronavirus outbreak.

‘We may see more mild cases as surveillance intensifies. So the issue is not so much on numbers that we know will go up,’ says Tarik Jašarević.



He adds: ‘There is no particular treatment, but it doesn’t mean that you cannot treat people’
