66 more suspected coronavirus cases as officials broaden list of symptoms 
 Hong Kong reported 66 more suspected cases of the Wuhan coronavirus over the night as a result of broader criteria set on people showing symptoms, bringing the total number of suspected cases to 236 as of 8am on Friday.Dr Chung Kin-lai, the Hospital Authority’s director of quality and safety, said the new cases were received from Friday midnight until 8am.
