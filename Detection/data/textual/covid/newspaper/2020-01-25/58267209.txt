China's State Council Information Office to hold a press briefing on virus on Jan. 26 
 BEIJING, Jan 25 (Reuters) - China’s State Council Information Office will hold a press briefing on the coronavirus outbreak on Sunday at 1500 local time, according to a notice published on its app. 
China’s National Health Commission Vice Minister Li Bin will give a briefing on the outbreak and answer questions, it said. (Reporting by Gabriel Crossley; editing by David Evans)
