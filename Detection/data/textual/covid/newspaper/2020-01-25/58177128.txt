WATCH LIVE: Chicago health officials talk about 2nd U.S. case of novel coronavirus 
 Physicians have diagnosed a second person in the U.S. with a confirmed case of novel coronavirus, federal health authorities said Friday. The patient is a woman in her 60s who lives in Chicago. She traveled to Wuhan, China, in December and returned to the U.S. on Jan. 13, according to officials with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, who also said she was not symptomatic while flying from China.
