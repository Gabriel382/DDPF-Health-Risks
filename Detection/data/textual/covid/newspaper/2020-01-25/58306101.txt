Coronavirus outbreak: Patients in France are in good condition, says public health chief 
 The three people confirmed to have contracted the Wuhan coronavirus in France are in good condition, the country's public health chief Jerome Salomon said on Saturday.



A day earlier, France confirmed the first three cases of the virus in Europe, with two patients hospitalized in Paris and the other in Bordeaux.
