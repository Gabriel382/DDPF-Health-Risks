Coronavirus claims 41 lives in China, 1,287 cases confirmed 
 China has confirmed 1,287 cases of patients infected with the new coronavirus as of January 24, while the death toll from the virus has risen to 41, the National Health Commission said on Saturday.The virus originated in the central Chinese city of Wuhan in Hubei late last year and has spread to Chinese cities including Beijing and Shanghai, as well as the United States, Thailand, South Korea and Japan.
