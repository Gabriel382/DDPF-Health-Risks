What health officials know about the coronavirus outbreak 
 As new cases of coronavirus continue to spread in China and around the world, a second patient in the United States was identified Friday as being infected with the virus. Dr. Edith Bracho-Sanchez, a pediatrician and assistant professor of pediatrics at Columbia University Irving Medical Center, joins Hari Sreenivasan to discuss the international outbreak.
