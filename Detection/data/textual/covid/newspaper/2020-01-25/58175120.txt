Coronavirus case confirmed in Victoria 
 Victorian Health Minister Jenny Mikakos has confirmed one case of coronavirus in the state.
Loading
The man is a Chinese National aged in his 50s and is being treated at monash in Clayton, Ms Mikakos said.
He arrived in Melbourne on January 15 from China and displayed no symptoms on flight to Melbourne, she said.
She said there was no cause for alarm for community, the patient is isolated and no other suspected cases.
