China confirms 1,287 coronavirus cases, with 41 deaths 
 BEIJING, Jan 25 (Reuters) - China has confirmed 1,287 cases of patients infected with the new coronavirus as of Jan. 24, while the death toll from the virus has risen to 41, the National Health Commission said on Saturday.

The virus originated in the central Chinese city of Wuhan in Hubei late last year and has spread to Chinese cities including Beijing and Shanghai, as well as the United States, Thailand, South Korea and Japan. (Reporting by Roxanne Liu, Judy Hua and Se Young Lee; Editing by Sandra Maler)
