        Tests in the UK on 31 people for the new strain of coronavirus have come back negative, the government has said.
"Across the UK there have been 31 concluded tests and no confirmed cases," a statement from the Department of Health said.
The coronavirus has killed at least 41 people and infected almost 1,300 since its discovery in the Chinese city of Wuhan.
