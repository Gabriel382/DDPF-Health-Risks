Carrie Lam declares coronavirus emergency in Hong Kong – video 
 Hong Kong’s chief executive, Carrie Lam, has raised the territory’s response to the coronavirus outbreak to emergency level.

Immigration and travel from Wuhan will be limited, major events cancelled and school openings delayed by two weeks. Lam said: ‘We have an emergency response level, it means that for this novel infectious disease, the impact on the population will be a high and imminent’
