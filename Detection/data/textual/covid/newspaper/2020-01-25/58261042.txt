France plans evacuation of French nationals from Wuhan, China - SCMP 
 (Reuters) - France is planning to evacuate French nationals trapped by the Chinese government's lockdown in Wuhan following the coronavirus outbreak, the South China Morning Post reported https://www.scmp.com/news/china/society/article/3047653/french-citizens-be-bused-out-wuhan-escape-coronavirus-consulate?utm_term=Autofeed&utm_medium=Social&utm_content=article&utm_source=Twitter#Echobox=1579953961 on Saturday, citing an email sent by the French consulate.
