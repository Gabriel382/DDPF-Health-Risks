Coronavirus spread: Britons trapped in Chinese city 
 Plans to evacuate hundreds of Britons trapped in China during the deadly coronavirus outbreak are being hampered by Beijing.
The Foreign Office was still waiting last night for permission to extract citizens from the city of Wuhan, which is under lockdown to contain the spread of the virus. Officials insisted that Beijing’s approval was only one of several obstacles to an evacuation, citing the risk of spreading the virus as a factor.
Military medics have been sent into hospitals in Wuhan
