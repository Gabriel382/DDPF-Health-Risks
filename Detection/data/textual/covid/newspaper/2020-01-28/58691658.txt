Hazzard: state schools will operate normally, private schools can issue whatever instructions they like 
 January 28, 2020. NSW Health Minister Brad Hazzard speaks to the media in Sydney saying private schools can do whatever they like in regards to restricting students regarding the coronavirus. The health minister says that government schools will be operating normally. Mr Hazzard added that if a child is showing signs of illness or if someone has had direct contact with someone who has contracted the virus, then they shouldn't come to school. "It normally takes two to fourteen days to manifest," Mr Hazzard says. (AAP Video/Michael Wade)
