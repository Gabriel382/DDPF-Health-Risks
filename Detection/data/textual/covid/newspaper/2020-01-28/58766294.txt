Coronavirus Tests Market’s Faith in Global Economy 
 Investors who began the year feeling largely sanguine about the stock market are struggling to make sense of whether a growing coronavirus outbreak could upend their bets on a global economic recovery.
 Just days ago, global stocks looked poised to close out January on a strong note. Benchmark indexes from the SP 500 to the Stoxx Europe 600 to India’s SP BSE Sensex climbed to records.
