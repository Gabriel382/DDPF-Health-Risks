More than 90 people tested for coronavirus in UK 
 A total of 97 people have been tested for coronavirus in the UK and all have been confirmed negative, the Department of Health has said.

    

            
              
                
                
                  











  
  
  
  






                
              
            

            
            
          
            
    More than 100 people who contracted the deadly virus have now died in China, and more than 4,500 cases have been identified globally.

    

            
              
                
                
                  



  


  












  
  
  
  






                
              
            

            
            
          
            
    Scientists have predicted it may already have arrived in the UK.
