Hazzard says the health system is working "extremely well" in regards to the coronavirus 
 January 28, 2020. NSW Health Minister Brad Hazzard speaks to the media in Sydney about the latest cases of the coronavirus in Sydney. A number of people have presented themselves to hospital and ten patients have been tested and all have been cleared. Today another six are being tested. "We are in a much better place now because GP's are much more aware of the issues," MR Hazzard says. The Health Minister says health the NSW and the Federal government health system is "working extremely well". (AAP Video/Michael Wade)
