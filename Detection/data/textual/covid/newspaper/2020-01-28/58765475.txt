Japan confirms coronavirus in man who had not been to China 
 TOKYO, Japan – Japanese authorities said on Tuesday, January 28, a man with no recent travel to China has contracted the novel strain of coronavirus – apparently after driving tourists from Wuhan, where a deadly outbreak began.

The man in his sixties from Nara drove two groups of tourists from Wuhan earlier in January and was hospitalised on Saturday with flu-like symptoms, the health ministry said. – Rappler.com
