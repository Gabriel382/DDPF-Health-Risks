Donald Trump considering China travel crackdown as deadly coronavirus spreads 
 White House officials are considering cracking down on flights between the US and China amid the burgeoning coronavirus crisis.

CNBC reported that the propsed restriction could affect flights into and out of China from the US, as well as airports across the country.

The restrictions were first mooted Monday, with coronavirus so far reported to have killed 106 people and infected a further 4,600.

It is believed to have originated at a live meat market in the Chinese city from Wuhan, and was first diagnosed late last month.
