‘A real pandemic would look more deadly’ than the coronavirus 
 Sky News host Andrew Bolt says the recent outbreak of the deadly coronavirus “sounds apocalyptic,” but “we seem so prone to panic”. Mr Bolt said there is “a lot of fake news” circulating online, and “racists have posted fake government alerts to avoid suburbs with lots of Chinese residents”. “Just five Australians have got this coronavirus, and none are seriously ill,” he said. “In Australia, 3,500 people die every year from the flu. Around the world, five million people die of the flu each year. “A real pandemic would look more deadly than this one.” Image: Associated Press
