Miami University students tested for coronavirus 
 Miami University officials are awaiting test results to determine whether two students who recently traveled to China are infected with a new strain of coronavirus, which can cause a deadly respiratory illness.

“Both students are isolated in their off-campus residences until their test results come back,” said Claire Wagner, spokeswoman for the school in the Butler County city of Oxford. “We are supporting them and their needs during this time.”
