Wuhan virus: China's Hubei province, centre of coronavirus outbreak, confirms 25 new deaths 
 BEIJING (REUTERS, AFP) - China's Hubei province, centre of a coronavirus outbreak that has spread throughout the country and overseas, on Wednesday (Jan 29) said another 25 patients had died in the province as a result of the virus and reported 840 new cases as of end-Tuesday.
