Africa on alert after Kenya reports suspected coronavirus patient 
 Airports across Africa are on high alert after a passenger arriving in Kenya on Tuesday was quarantined with coronavirus symptoms – the second suspected case on the continent.The student had travelled from Guangzhou to Nairobi on a Kenya Airways flight after spending time in the Chinese city of Wuhan, the epicentre of the virus outbreak.
