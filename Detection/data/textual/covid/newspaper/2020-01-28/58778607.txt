Britain finalising details for Wuhan evacuation: UK PM's spokesman 
 LONDON (Reuters) - Britain is urgently exploring options to for its nationals to leave the Chinese city of Wuhan, the centre of a coronavirus outbreak, and is finalising the details of the plan, a spokesman for Prime Minister Boris Johnson said on Tuesday. 
“The foreign office is urgently exploring options for British nationals to leave Hubei province, the details are being finalised,” the spokesman told reporters.
