Coronavirus outbreak: How to keep yourself safe | India News - Times of India 
 NEW DELHI: The Wuhan coronavirus has sickened almost 4,000 people and claimed 106 lives so far. Most of the deaths were reported in Wuhan, but officials have confirmed multiple deaths elsewhere, including the first in the capital Beijing. Macau, a gambling hub hugely popular with mainland tourists, has confirmed six cases as of January 27. In Hong Kong, eight people are known to have the disease. Of those, six arrived via a newly built high-speed train terminal that connects the city to the mainland. Here's a look at its global spread and how you can keep yourself safe.
