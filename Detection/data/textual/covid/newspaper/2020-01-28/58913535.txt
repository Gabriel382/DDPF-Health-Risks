Business daily - UK snubs US with green light for Huawei's 5G technology 
 The British government will allow Huawei to play a limited role in developing a 5G mobile network - in a snub to the Trump administration, which has been lobbying for the Chinese tech giant to be blocked. Also in the show - France's tourism industry worries about the impact of the coronavirus, and Renault's board of directors confirms a successor to the embattled Carlos Ghosn.
