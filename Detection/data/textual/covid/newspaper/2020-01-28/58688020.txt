China hit with face mask shortage amid coronavirus outbreak 
 Video obtained Tuesday, January 28, 2020, showing a number of Jiujiang residents lining up to purchase face masks direct from the factory amid Wuhan's coronavirus outbreak. China is facing a serious shortage of protective masks as the number of people infected with the deadly coronavirus continues to rise.
