Coronavirus concerns prompt Miami University to postpone basketball games 
 Because of concerns about the new strain of coronavirus, Miami University postponed the men’s basketball game scheduled for tonight against Central Michigan University and the women’s basketball game slated for Wednesday against Western Michigan University.

Miami University officials said they were awaiting test results to determine whether two of the school’s students who recently traveled to China are infected with a new strain of coronavirus, which can cause a deadly respiratory illness.
