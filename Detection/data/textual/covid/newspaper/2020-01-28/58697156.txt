China coronavirus: Hong Kong civil servants to be allowed to work from home 
 Most Hong Kong civil servants will be allowed to work from home for the rest of this week to minimise the threat of the Wuhan coronavirus spreading, the government declared on Tuesday as it urged the private sector to follow suit.The arrangement, which will not apply to those who provide urgent and necessary public services, will start from Wednesday to Sunday (Feb 2), pending a review by then.There are about 176,000 civil servants in Hong Kong.
