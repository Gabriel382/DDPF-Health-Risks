Person in Japan who has not visited Wuhan contracts coronavirus: minister 
 Pedestrians wearing masks stand in front of a crosswalk at a shopping district in Tokyo, Japan, January 28, 2020. REUTERS/Kim Kyung-Hoon


TOKYO (Reuters) - Japan health minister Katsunobu Kato said a person in Japan who had not visited Wuhan has contracted the new coronavirus, Jiji news agency reported on Tuesday.
