UPDATE 1-Person in Japan who has not visited Wuhan contracts coronavirus - minister 
 (Updates with details of infected person in second paragraph) 
TOKYO, Jan 28 (Reuters) - Japanese Health Minister Katsunobu Kato said a person in Japan who had not visited Wuhan has contracted the new coronavirus, Japanese media reported on Tuesday. 
The infected person is a tour bus driver in his 60s in the city of Nara, Kyodo said. 
Health Ministry officials were not immediately available for comment. (Reporting by Kevin Buckland; Editing by Andrew Heavens)
