Coronavirus: After Patna, suspected case in Jaipur; patient in isolation ward 
 A Coronavirus case has been suspected in Rajasthan’s Jaipur. A doctor who returned from China is under watch in a hospital. A few people were seen sporting masks in Rajasthan’s capital. Rajasthan minister Subhash Garg said that samples were sent to Pune. Earlier, a person was put under watch over Coronavirus fear in Patna. Ekta Kumari was admitted to Patna Medical College & Hospital. Kumari, who recently returned from China, claimed she was fine.
