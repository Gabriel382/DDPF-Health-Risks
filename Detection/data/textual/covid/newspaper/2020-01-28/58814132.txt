Bush Intercontinental Airport will screen passengers for coronavirus 
 This story is courtesy of our partners at KHOU 11. Click here for KHOU's story, related video and links to other stories about coronavirus.

HOUSTON — Even though there are no confirmed cases of the 2019 novel coronavirus in Texas, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said it will begin screening passengers at George Bush Intercontinental Airport for the strain.

IAH is one of 15 additional airports that will screen for the virus.
