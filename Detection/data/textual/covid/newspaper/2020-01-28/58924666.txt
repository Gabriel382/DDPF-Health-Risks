Will Virus Outbreak Derail Starbucks’s Strong Outlook? 
 Starbucks will have to wait and see if the coronavirus outbreak spoils its hot streak.
 The coffee giant affirmed its fiscal 2020 profit outlook on Tuesday as it reported first-quarter results. That guidance, which calls for total revenue and earnings growth of about 6% to 8%, comes with an asterisk: More than half of the company’s stores in China are currently closed due to the outbreak.
