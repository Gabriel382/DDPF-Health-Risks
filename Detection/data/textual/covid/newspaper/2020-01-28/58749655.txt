Person in Japan who has not visited Wuhan infected with coronavirus 
 TOKYO (REUTERS) - Japan's Health Minister Katsunobu Kato said a person in Japan who had not visited Wuhan city in China has contracted the new coronavirus, Jiji news agency reported on Tuesday (Jan 28).

Concerns are mounting over the coronavirus as health authorities in China reported a new surge in cases.

The health commission of Hubei province said on Tuesday that 106 people died, up from 81 the day before. The number of total confirmed cases in China surged to 4,515 as of Monday, from 2,835 the previous day, the National Health Commission said.
