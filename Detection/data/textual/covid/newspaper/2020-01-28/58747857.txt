China to halt individual visitors to Hong Kong to curb coronavirus 
 China will stop individual travellers to Hong Kong to curb the coronavirus spread, chief executive Carrie Lam said at a press conference on Tuesday.

Lam said all ferry services, and the high-speed rail service, will be suspended between Hong Kong and mainland China, according to Reuters. The number of flights will also be cut.

She also urged Hong Kong residents in China to return home.
