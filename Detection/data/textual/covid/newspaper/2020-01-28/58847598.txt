Pittsburgh companies shuffle to keep employees safe in China 
 Local companies with facilities in China, including a manufacturer with a plant in the hard-hit city of Wuhan, are monitoring the spread of the deadly coronavirus and trying to keep employees safe.

PPG (NYSE: PPG) has a coatings manufacturing plant in Wuhan, a city of 11 million that is the epicenter of the highly contagious respiratory disease that has killed more than 100 people and infected more than 2,500 people as of Tuesday.
