WHO Chief Praises Beijing’s Coronavirus Response as Travel Barriers Rise 
 The head of the World Health Organization praised China’s handling of the outbreak of a new and dangerous coronavirus during a visit to Beijing, as the U.S. raised its travel alert for the country to its second-highest warning level and other governments took steps to reduce exposure to travelers from China.
