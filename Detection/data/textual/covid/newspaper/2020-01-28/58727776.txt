Coronavirus update: NSW government urges children who have travelled to China in the past fortnight not to go to school 
 January 28, 2020. NSW Health Minister Brad Hazzard, the NSW Education Minister Sarah Mitchell, the NSW Chief Health Officer Kerry Chant and the Secretary of Education Mark Scott speak to the media in Sydney about children who have been to China in the past fortnight should stay home from school. "We are asking parents who have holidayed with their children in China, if they have not been back for 14 days, support the community by holding back your children." Mr Hazzard says.
