China halts travel permits for Chinese tourists going to Hong Kong 
 A customer walks past empty shelves at a supermarket following the outbreak of a new coronavirus, in Hong Kong, China, January 28, 2020. REUTERS/Tyrone Siu


BEIJING (Reuters) - China on Tuesday stopped issuing travel permits for Chinese tourists going to Hong Kong and Macau to help prevent the spread of the new coronavirus, state television reported.
