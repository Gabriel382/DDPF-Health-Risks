PM gives update on coronavirus 
 January 28, 2020. The Prime Minister Scott Morrison speaking at Blarney in Western NSW, about the coronavirus says there was a meeting in Beijing last night where embassy officials were briefed by the Chinese. The PM says he has recently spoken with the PM of New Zealand Jacinda Ardern to coordinate the return of nationals from Wuhan to Australia and New Zealand. The PM says the government is monitoring the situation closely. (AAP Video/supplied)
